Nick Knowles and the team enlist the help of the local community and guest designer Oliver Heath to transform a post-war prefab house in Dartford into a warm and safe home for the future. Single mum Louise had to stop work on her house when her son Thomas was diagnosed with Autistic Spectrum Disorder. Volunteers and the DIY SOS team spend nine gruelling days renovating Louise and Thomas's home.
