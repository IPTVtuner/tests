The 1850s and 60s were a time when overcrowded and unsanitary living conditions had reached their peak, leading to unprecedented outbreaks of disease. `Cure all' medicines that had promised to cure virtually everything, were all the rage and the team make their own out of rhubarb, liquorice, soap and syrup. They also venture into the uncertain world of electrotherapy.
