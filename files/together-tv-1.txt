Today, March 26
Sue and Baz in Cosham have a functional but bland garden that is in dire need of sprucing up. Baz works as an operating theatre technician and caught Covid-19, leaving him seriously ill for three months. During that time, Sue took solace in their garden. Now they want to create a tranquil Japanese-inspired space, a place of calm where Baz can continue his convalescence.
