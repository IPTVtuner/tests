In the last episode of the current series, Ade Edmondson is back in West Burton to see how shopkeeper Clare Gardiner is getting on. Earlier this summer, Clare scoured the Dales for bespoke local crafts and goods to stock the store which her partner Nigel built on the side of their house, and young farmer Will Wildman from Kirkby Malham travels to Middleton to buy some new sheep.
