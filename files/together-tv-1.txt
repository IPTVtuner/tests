Today, April 09
Sean and Beverly Lincoln are the happily married creators of a hit British TV show, who are persuaded to move to Los Angeles to remake it for American TV, with disastrous results. Things begin to unravel as soon as Sean and Beverly arrive in LA. It becomes clear that the network president has never even seen their show. To make matters worse, he insists that they replace their brilliant lead actor with Matt LeBlanc.
