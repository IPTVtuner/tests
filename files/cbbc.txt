Stan is a 15-year-old snooker player from Halifax who is on track to become a snooker champion. However, in order to turn professional, he has to win the next big UK tour. A tough task becomes more difficult when Stan he comes down with flu, pushing his dream further away than ever. This film follows Stan as he prepares to make his dream a reality, and sheds light on how professional snooker works.
