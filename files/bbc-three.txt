When Tamara found a suspicious invoice addressed to her husband, she knew something was up. She discovered that her husband had transferred large sums of money to a Mariam Mola from their joint account. And she soon realised Mariam had myriad other money-making schemes too. So began a dogged pursuit of Mariam, and to this film, in which Tamara joins forces with Mariam's other victims to expose her as the ruthless fraudster she really is.
