This episode tells the story of Gaia going missing from her aunt's house just before sunset on a November evening in 2017. Just before she leaves, she makes a last-minute call to her identical twin sister, Maya, then she runs. She's alone without a coat, phone or medication for her epilepsy and in a state of high distress. After making several emergency calls to police, Gaia's family believe that a widespread search for her is underway.
