Live basketball action comes from Dallas as the Mavericks face a tough test against defending NBA champions Denver Nuggets. The Nuggets won their first ever title last year with two-time regular season MVP Nikola Jokic central to their success. The Serbian is a hot favourite to again be named as the league's best player after another stellar season thus far. However, the Mavericks have intentions of going deep in the play-offs themselves.
