Sporting hero Freddie Flintoff returns to Preston with plans to take his team of unlikely lads on a cricket tour to India. But weeks before departure, a life-changing accident threatens to put a halt to their dreams. One year later, Freddie emerges back to the safety net of his team to announce the trip is back on, but once in India, the team must learn to live with each other and fend for themselves as they prepare to compete in one of the most revered cricketing nations in the world.
