The world's finest athletes face off in Morocco as the ever-enthralling Diamond League series continues with this season's fourth meeting. Some of athletics biggest stars shone in Rabat in last year's showing, with Norwegian superstar Jakob Ingebrigtsen triumphing in the 1500m in a season's best performance, while Jamaica's Shericka Jackson won the 200m in a meet record time.
