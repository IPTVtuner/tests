Trucking is big business. Cashing in on the boom is HGV driving school owner Shannan Paterson. She is on a mission to shake up the haulage scene and get more young drivers on the road. With an intense crash course, learners can bag their licence within five days and earn good money. Fun-loving Shannan is in the fast lane for success and has huge plans to supersize the business to keep up with demand.
