In the fourth and final week of heats, the celebrities up for the culinary challenge are TV presenter Kem Cetinay, double gold-medal-winning Paralympian Kadeena Cox, journalist Gavin Esler, Strictly Come Dancing's Johannes Radebe and actor Michelle Collins. The first challenge for the five hopefuls is the Under the Cloche test. In front of each celebrity is a cloche, underneath which is an ingredient.
