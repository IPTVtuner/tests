Jocelyn MacNab, Joshua Lester, Anna Acton, Chris Hannon, Tim Hibberd, Sally Howe. Live-action children's show following the everyday adventures of two fun-loving twins. Topsy and Tim are excited as they explore their new house. They love it but Tim thinks his new bedroom feels strange. It's bare and missing belongings from the room he used to share with Topsy. Dad has an idea how to make it more homely so at bedtime, the twins discover how to speak to each other from their separate bedrooms.
