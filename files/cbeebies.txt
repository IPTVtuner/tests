Jocelyn MacNab, Joshua Lester, Anna Acton, Suzy Aitchison, Abigail O'Brien, Marcus Korniotis. Live-action children's show following the everyday adventures of two fun-loving twins. The twins are excited because Grandma has given them brand new bikes with stabilisers. Tim finds it hard learning to ride his new bike and has a fall. Mummy gets her old bike from the garage and they all practice together.
