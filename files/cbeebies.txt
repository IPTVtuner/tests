Jocelyn MacNab, Joshua Lester, Anna Acton, June Whitfield, Tim Hibberd, Natalya Hibberd. Live-action children's show following the everyday adventures of two fun-loving twins. When Topsy wakes up, there is a lost cat sleeping on her bed! Topsy and Tim try to find out who the cat belongs to. They show a picture of the pet to people on their street but finding the animal's owner isn't as easy as the twins thought it would be.
