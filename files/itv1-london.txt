In Gino's Italy: Secrets Of The South popular TV chef Gino D'Acampo returns to his beloved home country on another food Odyssey, this time exploring the secret recipes of the little-known regions of Italy's far south. The series sees Gino travel from the fortress port of Gallipoli to remote mountainous region of Pollino, tasting some well-kept food secrets and learning to cook some traditional culinary delights. He explores the secrets of Puglia and Basilicata, a melting pot of history and culture.
