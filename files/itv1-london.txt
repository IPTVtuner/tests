ITV1 London
Today, June 13
Davina McCall hosts a very special programme of music and story-telling to commemorate the 80th anniversary of D-Day - the historic event will be told through a unique collection of letters and diary entries read by an array of invited guests and accompanied by the Royal Philharmonic Concert Orchestra. Also taking to this iconic stage are Emile Sande, Lulu, Katherine Jenkins and an exclusive performance from the cast of the Second World War musical Operation Mincemeat.
