Mark Pougatch presents coverage of the 2025 UEFA Men's Nations League Final in Munich - this is the fourth edition of the UEFA Nations League Finals and the showpiece will be contested between the winners of the semi-finals as hosts Germany take on inaugural champions Portugal and defending champions Spain face France, who were the winners of this competition in 2021.
