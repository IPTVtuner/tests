Agatha and Poirot marks 100 years since the first literary appearance of Agatha Christie's Belgian super sleuth, Hercule Poirot. Richard E Grant explores the life of Agatha, and the events that inspired the plots, characters, and locations of her many literary masterpieces. Along with some famous celebrity fans, Richard examines the relationship between Christie and her longest literary life companion.
