`The Tower II: Death Message', based on Kate London's second novel, sees Gemma Whelan and Jimmy Akingbola return as DS Sarah Collins and DC Steve Bradshaw, alongside BAFTA-nominated Tahirah Sharif as PC Lizzie Adams.
