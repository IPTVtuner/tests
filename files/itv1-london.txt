In Wakefield, Alan Titchmarsh, Frances Tophill and Danny Clarke show you how to create the definitive secret garden - full of bright and colourful plants, hidden corners and magical touches - for a wheelchair-bound little girl and her family. From a half-built castle folly, to a playful water feature and plant-petting corner, filled with sensory plants, this garden has everything to delight a young girl and her family.
