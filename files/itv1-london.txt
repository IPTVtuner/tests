Today, June 05
Steve Rider presents highlights of the third round of the 2024 Kwik-Fit British Touring Car Championship from Snetterton with reigning champion Ash Sutton leading the standings - Sutton is aiming for a record fifth BTCC title and has yet to win this season, but he has finished on the podium for all six races so far and is proving his class once again despite numerous regulation changes. Elsewhere, a resurgent Colin Turkington is two points behind after a brilliant performance at Brands Hatch.
