Mark Pougatch presents coverage of the 2023-24 FA Cup Fourth Round replay between Aston Villa and Chelsea in the company of Roy Keane and Karen Carney - after a 0-0 draw at Stamford Bridge in their first game, both teams meet again looking to seal their place in the quarter-finals. Villa last made it past this stage of the Cup in 2015 - that season saw them make it all the way to the final before losing 4-0 to Arsenal.
