Mark Pougatch presents coverage as England take on Greece in a crucial 2024-25 UEFA Nations League match in Athens. Greece beat England comprehensively at Wembley in September 2024 - as they top Group B2 having won all their Nations League matches, England need a result to have any chance of winning the group and avoiding a playoff in 2025. This will be the first match since the news was announced that Thomas Tuchel will be the next England manager - he takes over in January 2025.
