The five remaining make-up artists dive into the world of special effects make-up. This week's guest judge is visual artist Alexis Stone, famed for his mind-blowing transformations into a host of famous faces, from Lana Del Rey and Jennifer Coolidge to Mrs Doubtfire and Cruella Deville. For their industry assignment, they are on the set of legendary medical drama Casualty.
