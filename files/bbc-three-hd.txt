Eight young renters facing eviction have just weeks to figure out how to fight for their rights or find a new place to call home. With a thousand eviction notices issued every week, they are not alone, though it can feel like it. Soaring rents and a lack of properties make finding a new place seem impossible. So what are their options? And how will they face the biggest fear of all - homelessness?
