Today, November 30
Live action from the final of South America's premier club competition as Atletico Mineiro face Botafogo at the historic El Monumental stadium in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Atletico Mineiro last lifted this trophy in 2013 and knocked out previous winners Fluminense and Argentinian giants River Plate en route to this final. Opponents Botafogo have never reached this stage of the competition before and have a number of former Premier League players in their ranks.
