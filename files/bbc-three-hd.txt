BBC Three HD
Today, September 17
Live coverage of the final evening of Diamond League action this year, as the enthralling competition closes with a bang in Eugene, Oregon. Among the stellar athletes on show there are plenty of Brits, with Dina Asher-Smith and Zharnel Hughes both competing in the 200m and Keely Hodgkinson in the 800m. The world-class field also includes the likes of Jamaican sprinter Shericka Jackson, American speedsters Noah Lyles and Erriyon Knighton, and Swedish superstar pole vaulter Mondo Duplantis.
