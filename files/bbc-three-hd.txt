BBC Three HD
Today, October 19
Live coverage of the final session on the penultimate night of action at the Track Cycling World Championships in Ballerup, Denmark. In the women's madison, Great Britain will have high hopes of gold, while the medals are also decided in the individual pursuit. In the one men's title tonight, Portugal's Olympic madison champion Iuri Leitao will hope to defend the omnium title he won last year. Commentary comes from Simon Brotherton and Chris Boardman.
