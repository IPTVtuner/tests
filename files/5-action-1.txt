Today, July 16
Every day, 3.2 billion litres of wastewater end up in the British sewer system. This would be enough to fill more than 1,500 Olympic swimming pools. Dean Webster and his cleaning team descend into the underworld in Cumbria to clean a sewer. Meanwhile, Neil Golightly and his colleague Jonny Fields are deployed to Blackpool to fix some blocked pipes.
