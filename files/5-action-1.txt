Martial arts extravaganza in which twin brothers, both expert fighters, are reunited after many years to avenge their parents' murder. After the killing, engineered by their father's unscrupulous business partner, the young sons are separated. One becomes a Hong Kong smuggler, the other a Beverly Hills aerobics instructor, but despite their very different lifestyles, they are united in the desire for justice.
