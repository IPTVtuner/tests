BBC Red Button 1
Today, April 06
The huge hits and high-octane action of rugby league's premier cup competition continues as Hull FC host fierce city rivals Hull KR in the quarter-finals. This will be the first time the Hull derby has taken place in the cup since 1986, and hosts Hull FC's confidence will be high after the Black and Whites knocked out the all-conquering Wigan Warriors in a sensational second-half comeback to reach the last eight.
