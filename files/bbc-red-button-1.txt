Today, May 22
Warm up for the 2023/24 FA Cup final at Wembley by watching how Premier League giants and non-league minnows fared in the latest campaign of football's oldest cup competition. This season's FA Cup has been packed with giant-killing journeys and underdog exploits, as sixth-tier Maidstone United became the lowest ranked team since 1978 to reach the fifth round. There was heartbreak aplenty, including Coventry City's semi-final defeat on penalties.
