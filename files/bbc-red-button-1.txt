Tanya Arnold presents highlights from the latest round of matches in Super League. With just four rounds to go, it's Catalans Dragons who lead the way, and they're looking to maintain their lead at the top of the table when they visit Hull KR. At the other end of the table the battle to avoid relegation continues with Wakefield at home to St Helens and Castleford travelling to Warrington. Wigan play Salford, Hull FC face Leeds and Challenge Cup winners Leigh take on Huddersfield.
