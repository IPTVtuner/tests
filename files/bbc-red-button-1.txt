Today, June 01
Matt Baker hosts coverage of the men's vault, horizontal and parallel bar finals, and women's balance beam and floor finals at the European Championships in Leipzig, Germany. Great Britain's Jake Jarman is the defending champion in the men's vault competition, while Italian star and Olympic gold medallist Alice D'Amato is the one to watch in the women's beam. Expert analysis is provided by Olympic bronze medallist Beth Tweddle.
