Today, May 11
Rishi Persad presents from stunning south Gloucestershire as the Badminton Horse Trials reach their climax with the enthralling show jumping tests. The globally renowned trials take place in the grounds of Badminton House - the seat of the Duke of Beaufort - and feature dressage, cross-country and jumping across several days. In 2024, New Zealand's Caroline Powell was a shock overall winner at the age of 51.
