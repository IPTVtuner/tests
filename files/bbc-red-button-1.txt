BBC Red Button 1
Today, October 24
Mark Chapman presents highlights from the third round of matches in Europe's elite club competition as the five British clubs once again face tricky fixtures. Liverpool have a difficult trip to Germany to take on RB Leipzig, Celtic are at Europa League champions Atalanta and Manchester City take on Sparta Prague. There is also the best of the action from Tuesday's fixtures, in which Arsenal hosted Shakhtar Donetsk and Aston Villa welcomed Bologna to Villa Park.
