BBC Red Button 1
Today, September 14
A star-studded line-up, packed with champions and medallists from the 2024 Olympic Games, is in action at the 2024 Diamond League final in Brussels. Olympic 100m champion Julien Alfred will be putting her status as the world's fastest woman on the line, competing in both the 100m and 200m, in which she took gold and silver respectively in Paris 2024. Also in action at the famous King Baudouin Stadium are Jakob Ingebrigtsen, who won 5,000m gold at the 2024 Olympics.
