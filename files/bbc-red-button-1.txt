BBC Red Button 1
Today, May 20
The final slot in this season's Challenge Cup final is at stake as 2022 finalists Huddersfield Giants take on nine-time winners Warrington Wolves in St Helens. In the second men's Challenge Cup semi-final of the weekend, Huddersfield will be attempting to reach the final for the second time in three years after shocking Catalans Dragons in Perpignan in the last round.
