Highlights as the UEFA Champions League's new revamped league format continues as the first round of fixtures are completed. England are represented this season by Manchester City, Arsenal, Liverpool and newcomers Aston Villa. Holders Real Madrid, now boasting superstar striker Kylian Mbappe alongside the likes of Jude Bellingham and Vinicius Jnr, are beginning their quest to lift the trophy for a record-extending 16th time.
