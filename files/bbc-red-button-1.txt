BBC Red Button 1
Today, June 07
Tanya Arnold presents coverage as St Helens and Wigan Warriors do battle for the Women's Challenge Cup at Wembley Stadium. The Wigan-Saints rivalry is one of the most famous in rugby league, but this will be the first time the women's teams have met in a major final. Saints have been the dominant force in an ever-expanding women's game and are aiming to lift the trophy for the fifth consecutive time.
