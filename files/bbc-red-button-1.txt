Today, May 18
All the goals and drama from Wembley on the most celebrated day of England's football calendar as Crystal Palace, seeking a first major trophy, faced much-decorated Manchester City. City are contesting the final for the third successive year, as many times as Palace have managed in their entire 120-year history. But with the attacking threat of Eberechi Eze, Ismaila Sarr and Jean-Philippe Mateta, can the Eagles soar to victory?
