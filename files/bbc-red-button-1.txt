BBC Red Button 1
Today, June 12
Live coverage as a host of athletics stars, including Mondo Duplantis and Karsten Warholm, compete in Oslo in the sixth leg of the 2025 Diamond League. A sell-out crowd at the famous Bislett Stadium will be hoping Olympic pole vault champion Duplantis can once again set a new world record. Meanwhile, world champion and former Olympic 400m hurdles champion Warholm will have the home support as he takes on Rai Benjamin.
