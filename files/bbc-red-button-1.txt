BBC Red Button 1
Today, November 05
JJ Chalmers presents coverage as England's world champions face off against France in a mouth-watering rematch of last year's World Cup final. The two nations played out an enthralling contest in November, when England overcame a half-time deficit to prevail 28-24 on home soil. Captain Tom Halliwell scored the decisive try with just three minutes left on the clock. The rematch takes place at Leeds Arena.
