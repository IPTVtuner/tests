BBC Red Button 1
Today, May 26
Live coverage of the fourth Diamond League meeting of the 2025 season, with many of the world's best athletes in action in Morocco. Kenyan prodigy Emmanuel Wanyonyi won the Olympic 800m title in Paris and says that this year he is targeting the world record set by compatriot David Rudisha at the 2012 London Games. Another Kenyan star due to be in attendance is double Olympic champion Beatrice Chebet, who won over both 5,000m and 10,000m and is also the record holder in the latter discipline.
