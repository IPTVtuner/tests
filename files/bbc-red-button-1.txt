Today, October 03
Mark Chapman presents highlights from the second round of games in Europe's elite club competition, including the eagerly anticipated match between Aston Villa and Bayern Munich. The match at Villa Park is a repeat of the 1982 European Cup final, won 1-0 by the English side. Six-time winners Liverpool are also playing on Wednesday, hosting Italian club Bologna at Anfield, while there is also the best of the action from Tuesday's fixtures.
