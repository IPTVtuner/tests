BBC Red Button 1
Today, August 25
Coverage from Silesia, Poland, as the thrilling Diamond League series continues with a host of the stars from the 2024 Olympic Games. 2023's event saw many of the top names in athletics excel, with South Africa's Wayde van Niekerk winning the men's 400m, while the women's 100m race was won by Sha'Carri Richardson, who won an Olympic silver medal in Paris. Norway's Jakob Ingebringtsen is also back competing in the men's 1500m after missing out on an Olympic medal.
