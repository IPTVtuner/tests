BBC Red Button 1
Today, December 01
Can seventh-tier side Kettering Town pull off a second successive giant-killing win over league opposition and earn a place in the FA Cup third round for the first time in 16 years? The Poppies came from behind to earn a shock victory away to League One neighbours Northampton Town in the previous round, courtesy of goals from local boy Luca Miller and former Newcastle United striker Nile Ranger.
