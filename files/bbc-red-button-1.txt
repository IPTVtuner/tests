Today, May 30
Matt Baker hosts coverage of the men's all-around final at the European Championships in Leipzig, Germany, with expert analysis from Olympic bronze medallist Beth Tweddle. Great Britain have sent a strong team to Germany, featuring Olympic medallists Jake Jarman and Harry Hepworth. The pair helped Britain win team silver at last year's European Championships, while Jarman won the individual all-around silver medal in 2023.
