Every year, North Yorkshire villagers battle it out to see who can grow the biggest onion. The Windmill Onion Club has been running smoothly in Stainsacre for two decades, but this year, a fresh face attempts to disrupt the status quo. Journalist Adam Clarkson meets the characters, documents the highs and the lows and follows the local underdog, his dad, Richard.
