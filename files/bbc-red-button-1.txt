BBC Red Button 1
Today, May 31
Live coverage from Oslo, Norway, as the world's best athletes meet in the latest instalment of this season's Diamond League series. Last season's meeting saw home favourites Karsten Warholm and Jakob Ingebrigtsen thrill the Norwegian crowd with scintillating performances. Warholm set a new Diamond League record in the 400m hurdles, while Olympic 1500m champion Ingebrigtsen ran a European record time to take victory to the delight of the fans in the Bislett Stadium.
