BBC Red Button 1
Today, May 03
A host of world-class athletes descend on Shanghai for the second Diamond League meeting of 2025. Among the headline acts competing are Botswana's Olympic 200m champion Letsile Tebogo, who is taking on a stacked field in the 100m, while another Paris gold medallist in USA's Grant Holloway will as ever be the man to beat over the 110m hurdles. Another star name in action is world, Olympic and reigning Diamond League high jump champion Yaroslava Mahuchikh of Ukraine.
