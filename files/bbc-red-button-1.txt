BBC Red Button 1
Today, September 29
Live coverage from Switzerland of the men's road race as the best cyclists on the planet battle it out over 274km to claim the prestigious rainbow jersey. Last year it was Dutchman Mathieu van der Poel who crossed the line first, with Belgium's Wout van Aert second and Slovenia's Tadej Pogacar ending third. Presented by JJ Chalmers, with commentary from Simon Brotherton, Chris Boardman and Hayley Simmonds.
