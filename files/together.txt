Ruth Watson advises the owners of stately British homes how to diversify and raise revenue, to secure the future of their unique properties for generations to come. Fifteen years ago Alastair and Mary-Anne Robb bought Cothay Manor and worked tirelessly to restore the house to its former glory. To try to raise revenue they opened their beautiful gardens to the public, but unfortunately, they've never made a profit.
