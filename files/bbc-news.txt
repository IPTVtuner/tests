BBC News
Today, September 15
Talking Movies travels to the Venice and Telluride film festivals to report on some of the key films that have been animating audiences and making an impact early in the awards season. From Venice, Emma Jones reports on the adulation actor Angelina Jolie received for her portrayal of the celebrated late opera star Maria Callas in the picture Maria. In Telluride, Tom Brook hears from UK film-maker Kevin Macdonald, who has put together One to One: John & Yoko.
