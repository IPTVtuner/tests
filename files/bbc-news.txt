BBC News
Today, October 27
Talking Movies reports from the New York Film Festival - one of the most prestigious film events in North America. Among the highlights covered by the team are celebrated Spanish film-maker Pedro Almodovar's first English-language full-length feature The Room Next Door and Mike Leigh's Hard Truths, starring Marianne Jean-Baptiste, who previously worked with Leigh in his 1996 picture Secrets & Lies.
