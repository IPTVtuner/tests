Arts in Motion is an exhilarating new series that brings together celebrated cultural icons with the next generation of artists. In the second episode, five young drama students experience an unforgettable masterclass led by the legendary Brian Cox, renowned for his powerful performances on stage and screen - and, of course, as the formidable Logan Roy in Succession. Returning to his old drama school of LAMDA in London, Brian shares his deep passion for nurturing talent.
