Sky Arts
Today, October 24
Hosts Joan Bakewell and Frank Skinner head out on another search for the Portrait Artist of the Year in the third season of this competition series. This time around the contest includes over 20 celebrity sitters including Imelda Staunton, Tom Courtenay, Richard E Grant, Sue Perkins, Dave Myers and Parminder Nagra. In each round the competing artists have just four hours to finish their masterpiece before it is assessed by the judges, Tai-Shan Schierenberg, Kathleen Soriano and Kate Bryan. The overall winner of the show is presented the title and a prestigious commission.
