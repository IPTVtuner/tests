Sky Arts
Today, November 12
Hosts Frank Skinner and Joan Bakewell embark on a new search for the United Kingdom's next star portrait artist. On hand to judge the artworks produced by the contestants are the judging panel, which features art historian and curator Kate Bryan, contemporary art expert Kathleen Soriano, and acclaimed portrait painter Tai-Shan Schierenberg. Celebrities that sit for portraits in season four include actor David Tennant, comedian Noel Fielding, actress Michaela Coel, and television presenter Reggie Yates.
