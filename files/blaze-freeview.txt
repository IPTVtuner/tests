In the final episode Dr Alice Roberts goes in search of the Tudor age, a time that saw momentous changes across all aspects of British life. Along the way Alice visits excavations at Shakespeare's first theatre in London's Shoreditch, where the Bard began his career and `Romeo and Juliet' was first performed. Alice also joins a team sifting through Shakespeare's rubbish at his last home in Stratford-Upon-Avon.
