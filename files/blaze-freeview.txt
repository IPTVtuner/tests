Today, May 20
Tatton Park in Cheshire plays host to David Dickinson and the team as they size up a mix of desirable antiques and vintage ornaments. Stuart Hofgartner is sent into a spin when an unusual car mascot lands on his table and Alison Chapman sets her sights on a Thomas George Cooper painting. At auction, a Masonic gold ball fetches an excellent price.
