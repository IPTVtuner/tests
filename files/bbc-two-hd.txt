Today, June 08
Tanya Arnold introduces live coverage from Wembley where St Helens take on Leeds Rhinos, with Saints bidding to win the Women's Challenge Cup for a fourth consecutive year. The teams have met in the last two finals, with the game held at Wembley for the first time in 2023. The quality of that match lived up to the historic occasion, with both sides showcasing their attacking styles, but it was Saints who eventually triumphed, winning 22-8.
