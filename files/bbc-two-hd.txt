A celebration of R&B superstar Mary J Blige that spans the highlights of her groundbreaking career and features the very best of her appearances on a selection of BBC shows. From the early songs of the mid-1990s right up to her acclaimed 2015 performance on Glastonbury's Pyramid Stage, this collection demonstrates why Mary has been labelled as 'the queen of hip hop soul', featuring such hits as Just Fine, Everything and No More Drama.
