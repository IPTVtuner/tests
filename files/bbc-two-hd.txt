Highlights from day three at the European Tour's traditional flagship event at Wentworth's famous West Course. Presented by Eilidh Barbour. Among the world's top golfers taking part is four-time major champion Rory McIlroy, who is vying for his second title here after claiming the trophy in 2014. McIlroy was a runner-up last year, tied for second place on 16-under with world number three Jon Rahm, as both men finished one shot behind the winner Shane Lowry.
