Katherine Downes presents live grass-court tennis as the world's leading female players take to the pristine courts of Eastbourne. The Devonshire Park venue has been graced by many greats of the game, with former winners including Martina Navratilova, Monica Seles and Justine Henin. 2024 sees a number of the world's top 10 competing, including 2022 Wimbledon champion Elena Rybakina, as they seek to fine tune their game ahead of a trip to SW19.
