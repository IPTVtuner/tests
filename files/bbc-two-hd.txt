Following the attacks of 7 October 2023, Israel has vowed to destroy Hamas. But can it? For decades, Hamas has been building and funding a global network in opposition to Israel and its existence. With exclusive access to some of Hamas' most closely guarded financial secrets, John Ware investigates Hamas' network outside Gaza, uncovering a web of financial enablers, as well as UK-based supporters and cheerleaders.
