The Ardern family run a shop in the 1920s, 30s and 40s. It's the golden age of the corner shop, when professional grocery skills are at their height, and shop keeping is seen as a science. Ardern & Son supplies almost everything its local customers could need and new branded goods like Cadbury's Dairy Milk to Heinz Tomato Ketchup are jostling for space on the shelves.
