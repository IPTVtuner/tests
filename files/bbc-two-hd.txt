An emotional journey through the Hairy Bikers story as Si King celebrates the life of best mate and former TV partner Dave Myers. It follows Si as he leads a motorcycle convoy to Dave's hometown of Barrow-in-Furness, while archive material, new interviews and previously unseen footage help celebrate Dave's life, charting a journey that took him around the world while cooking up a storm as one of television's greatest ever double acts.
