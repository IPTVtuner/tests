American superstar Simone Biles and Britain's Jessica Gadirova are among the big names set to compete in the women's all-around final in Antwerp. After a two-year break, Biles made a stunning return to in August 2023, winning the US national title in thrilling style. Gadirova made history in 2022 by becoming the first British woman to win an all-around medal at the World Championships when she won bronze in Liverpool.
