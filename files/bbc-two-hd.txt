Jill Douglas presents live coverage as the Track Cycling World Championships comes to a thrilling conclusion, with gold medals up for grabs in five events. In the women's keirin, Great Britain's Emma Finucane will try and get the better of her New Zealand foe Ellesse Andrews. The Kiwi claimed golds in the Paris Olympics in both the keirin and sprint, with Finucane having to settle for bronze in both events.
