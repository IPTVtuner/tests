The Repair Shop team bring their expertise to a host of cherished objects that have travelled the globe, from a camera that documented the French army in Vietnam to a suitcase from a small town in Northern Ireland. On expert Dean Westmoreland's to-do list is a sparkling restoration on a pair of light-up high heels. The glittery shoes, brought to the barn by Nathan from York, are the first pair he ever bought for his drag act alter ego 'Bailey Bubbles'.
