Danny Sebastian celebrates the eclectic and nostalgic world of collecting in a special episode of the programme. He picks out some of the most popular names in collectibles, from silver and ceramics to glassware and mid-century toys, and shares his insider tips to help you spot the best bargains. Joining him is Craig Glenday, editor-in-chief of Guinness World Records, to discuss some of the world's biggest and wackiest collections.
