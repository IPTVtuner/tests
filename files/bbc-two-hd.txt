The human world, from our cities to our fields, has spread to every corner of the globe. Wildlife must now adapt to a vastly altered landscape, and contend directly with us. Some animals are meeting this challenge head-on and thriving. In Bali, long-tailed macaques have learned to steal tourists' most precious items and then use them to barter with temple staff for their favourite food. The older males simply won't give you back your phone unless they get what they're after.
