Tanya Arnold presents highlights from the first weekend of matches in the 2023 Super League play-offs. After 27 rounds of the league season, the competition becomes a knock-out event as the top six teams battle it out for a place in the grand final at Old Trafford, where the Super League champions will be crowned. Week one features two games involving the teams who finished in third to sixth places in the Eliminators.
