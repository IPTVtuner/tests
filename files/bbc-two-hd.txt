Ugo Monye introduces highlights from an action-packed first weekend of the 2024 Six Nations. The action begins in Marseille with the heavyweight clash between tournament favourites France and last year's Grand Slam winners Ireland. Italy then host England, before the final match sees Scotland travel to Cardiff to take on Wales. Ugo is joined for expert analysis of the major talking points and incidents by former Wales and Lions captain Sam Warburton and ex-Scotland skipper John Barclay.
