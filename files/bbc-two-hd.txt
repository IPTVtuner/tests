Claire Duffy wanted the best in life for her young family, especially youngest daughter Abbie, who had recently received a cystic fibrosis diagnosis. She was sure that the warm weather and outdoor lifestyle of New Zealand would do wonders for Abbie's health, but had to convince home bird husband Russell that moving halfway across the world was a viable option. Two years later, have they made the leap, or was the pull of home just too strong?
