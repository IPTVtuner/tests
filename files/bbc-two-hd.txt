Leading criminal barristers Sasha Wass QC and Jeremy Dein QC explore a notorious case of a house servant accused of murdering his upper-class employer. Devon, 1884. In the early hours of a cold November morning, in picturesque Babbacombe Bay, servants at the Glen manor house awoke to the smell of smoke. They found their villa engulfed by flames and realised that their upper-class employer Emma Keyse was missing.
