In April 1999, one of Britain's most celebrated and loved television presenters and newsreaders, Jill Dando, was shot and killed on the doorstep of her home in the middle of the day. It was a crime that reverberated across the country, from the millions of television viewers used to seeing Jill in their living rooms, all the way up to the heart of government, even the Queen commented on Jill's death.
