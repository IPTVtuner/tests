Nadiya Hussain continues her tour of America's culinary melting pot in California. She starts just a stone's throw from downtown Los Angeles, in a Latino neighbourhood, home to an authentic Central American night market. Here she find the country's newest arrivals, on the first step of their American dream, cooking up a taste of home: delicious Guatemalan stuffed chilies and, from El Salvador, thick corn tortillas with spicy salsa.
