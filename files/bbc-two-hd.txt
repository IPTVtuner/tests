Flamboyant frills, huge collars and puffed sleeves are the hallmarks of Tudor tailoring, and in this episode, Natasha meets historical costumier Ninya Mikhaila to learn about 16th-century fashions. Together, they unwrap a genuine Farthingale sleeve to see how it was made. Historic items are also on the shopping lists of both teams at Newark antiques fair. Car sales executives Sam and Jake are the red team. They reckon 'negotiation is the key'.
