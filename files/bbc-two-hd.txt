It's Volunteers' Week, and as part of a nationwide event The Big Help Out, Natasha Raskin Sharp presents an inspirational episode that's all about volunteering. At Newark fair in Nottinghamshire, two teams of young volunteers are shopping for antiques, with experts Danny Sebastian and Kate Bliss lending a hand. On the red team are Nancy and Ellen, who help out at a local foodbank and a historic visitor attraction.
