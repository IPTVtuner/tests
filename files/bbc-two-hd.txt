St Helens seek to begin the Super League season with a win for the 12th year in a row, although Paul Rowley's well-drilled Salford Red Devils will provide a tough challenge. Salford finished fourth in the regular season last year, two places above St Helens, and recently put aside ongoing financial issues to win 40-12 away to Saints in a pre-season testimonial.
