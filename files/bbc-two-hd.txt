Satirical `rockumentary' following ageing British heavy metal band Spinal Tap - David St Hubbins, Nigel Tufnel, Derek Smalls and their latest hapless drummer - on the comeback trail, promoting their album `Smell the Glove' on a US tour. The film features many moments that have passed into legend, including exploding drummers and amplifiers that go up to 11.
