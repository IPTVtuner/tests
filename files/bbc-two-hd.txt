Today, June 02
The world's best athletes arrive in Stockholm, Sweden, for the seventh round of this year's Diamond League series. Kyron McMaster, a previous back-to-back Diamond League champion, will go up against 2022 series winner Alison Dos Santos in the men's 400m hurdles, while the USA's Fred Kerley and World Championship silver medallist Letsile Tebogo of Botswana will face off in the 100m.
