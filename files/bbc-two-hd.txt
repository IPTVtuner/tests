Since 1947, the International Musical Eisteddfod in Llangollen has hosted more than 400,000 competitors from all over the world. In this show, Roo Irvine discovers the popularity of the artistic competition and finds out how some very well-known names have all left their mark on the event. Meanwhile, the red and blue teams are hoping to upstage each other as they hit the stalls at Oswestry Showground on the border between England and Wales.
