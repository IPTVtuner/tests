Today, November 12
David Dimbleby presents highlights of the National Service of Remembrance as the King leads the commemorations at the Cenotaph in London. His Majesty is joined on Whitehall by senior members of the royal family and leading UK politicians, alongside the armed forces, representatives of the Commonwealth and faith communities for the morning's commemorations. The two-minute silence at 11 o'clock is followed by the wreath laying as the nation remembers all those who died serving their country.
