Off the southern coast of mainland Greece lies the oldest submerged city in the world, Pavlopetri. Led by underwater archaeologist Dr Jon Henderson, an international team of experts attempt to prise age-old secrets from the complex of streets and stone buildings laying less than five metres below the surface. State-of-the-art CGI helps to raise the city from the seabed for the first time in 3500 years.
