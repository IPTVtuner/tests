Leading criminal barristers Jeremy Dein QC and Sasha Wass QC reinvestigate a case of matricide dating from the 1920s, in which a renowned fraudster went to the gallows accused of murdering his mother. Kent, 1929. On the evening of 23 October, in the coastal town of Margate, a distraught guest of the Hotel Metropole stormed downstairs into the lobby. That guest was Sydney Fox. He screamed that his mother's room was on fire, and begged for help.
