Season three of the documentary series 'Secrets of the World's Most Expensive Cruise Ship' follows the cruise ship 'Seven Seas Splendor', its passengers, and crew on the ship's inaugural Northern Europe season going from Norway to Denmark. Each episode deals with a different theme such as how to contend with an unexpected sea day, what it takes to pull off a vows renewal at sea, and personal stories from crew members who work tirelessly to give passengers an unforgettable cruise experience.
