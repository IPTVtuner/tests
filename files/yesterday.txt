Today, May 06
Fiona Bruce presents a special episode of Antiques Roadshow commemorating the 80th anniversary of the Battle of Britain and the Blitz. Combining stories from all over the country with a special event filmed at Biggin Hill airfield, the programme tells the story of a defining moment in Britain's war through the people and objects who were there. Fiona begins the programme onboard an iconic Supermarine Spitfire, flying over the Weald of Kent.
