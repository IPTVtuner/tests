Today, September 18
As part of its 40th anniversary series, Antiques Roadshow arrives on the set of `EastEnders' for a special episode celebrating the history of film, music, theatre and television. Set against the backdrop of Albert Square at the BBC's Elstree studios in north London, the Antiques Roadshow team appraise a selection of rare and unusual items of entertainment memorabilia owned by members of the public.
