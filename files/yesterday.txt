Today, January 03
Just 15 miles from the bustle of Birmingham, Fiona Bruce and the team visit the tranquil moated manor house of Baddesley Clinton. A busy day of valuations uncovers more fascinating finds including a gruesome box containing a long wooden peg removed from a child's eye by a surgeon in the 1780s, a glittering diamond bracelet once worn by Hollywood star Rita Hayworth, plus a painting made by Lord Mountbatten of his family home.
