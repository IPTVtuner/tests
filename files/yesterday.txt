Compilation of classic archive performances from the guitar gods of the late 60s and 70s. Status Quo appear playing `Pictures of Matchstick Men' on `Top of the Pops' in 1968, the Who perform `Long Live Rock' in the Old Grey Whistle Test studio, Dire Straits play `Tunnel of Love' and Lynyrd Skynyrd bring a taste of the south with `Sweet Home Alabama'.
