Today, June 14
Fiona and the team head to Suffolk to Helmingham Hall that has been home to twenty generations of the Tollemache family. The moated Tudor manor house, with two working draw bridges, is the perfect backdrop for a roadshow brimming with antique finds. Lee Young discovers a collection of seventeenth and eighteenth century decorative boxes that the owner is using as his pension fund. Militaria specialist Bill Harriman is intrigued by a mediaeval sword dredged up from a pond on a golf course.
