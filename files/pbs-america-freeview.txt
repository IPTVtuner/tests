They defined music and popular culture like no other band ever will, but how did The Beatles make the journey from Merseyside teenagers to international pop stars in the 1960s? This is the story of how American rock `n' roll and rhythm and blues dragged post-war Liverpool into one of the most vibrant music cities ever with the Mersey Sound. Featuring unique archives and revealing interviews from those involved in the early years of The Beatles in Liverpool and Hamburg.
