Grand Tours of Scotland returns for a third series to take viewers on six journeys to relive the golden days of Scottish tourism. The first episode sees presenter Paul Murton travel round one of Scotland's best loved holiday destinations, the Isle of Arran. Known as `Scotland in miniature', Paul sets of to explore this diverse island that has something for everyone; ruined castles and rugged mountains.
