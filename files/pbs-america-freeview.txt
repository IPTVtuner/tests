Jules Hudson follows a deep water dive team searching for the shipwrecks of WWII's Battle of the Atlantic off the coast of Northern Ireland. In this edition, the dive team discover the wreck of an Allied escort vessel sunk 40 miles off of Donegal as well as the wrecks of the German U-boats that prowled Northern Irish coastlines. Jules Hudson uncovers the remains of a massive US military base.
