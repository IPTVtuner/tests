Today, February 25
Clara Amfo introduces a prom which celebrates the jazz and blues legend Nina Simone. The Metropole Orkest is led by Jules Buckley and they will be joined by Ledisi, Lisa Fischer and other singers in this tribute to the acclaimed singer, songwriter and political activist. The titles will include standards such as `Feeling Good', `My Baby Just Cares for Me' and `I Put a Spell on You'.
