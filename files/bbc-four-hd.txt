On Christmas Eve 1975, Queen crowned a glorious year with a special concert at London's Hammersmith Odeon. This final night of their triumphant UK tour has become a legendary event in Queen's history. Featuring stunning renditions of early hits `Keep Yourself Alive', `Liar' and `Now I'm Here' alongside Brian May's epic `Brighton Rock' and a rip-roaring version of the classic `Bohemian Rhapsody'.
