A look at the early female movie stars Greta Garbo, Marlene Dietrich, Rita Hayworth, Marilyn Monroe; immortal goddesses made by Hollywood to reign over the silver screen. From the power of Joan Crawford to the seductiveness of Rita Hayworth, Hollywood studios produced their own brand of beautiful, sassy and confident women. The era drew to a close with the supreme fame of Elizabeth Taylor and the tragic death of Marilyn Monroe.
