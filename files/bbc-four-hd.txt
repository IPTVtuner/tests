After victory at the Battle of St Alban's, Plantaganet and the Yorkists force Henry VI and the Lancastrians to give away their right to the Crown on Henry's death. Queen Margaret is outraged that her son should lose his inheritance. Margaret attacks and murders Plantaganet at his house. His surviving sons, Edward, George and Richard, escape. Joined by the Duke of Warwick, they take up the struggle against the Queen and the House of Lancaster.
