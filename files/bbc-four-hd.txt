First broadcast in 1983, Grey Granite was the third and final part of Lew Grassic Gibbon's A Scots Quair trilogy that the BBC adapted fortelevision. Leading actress Vivien Heilbron looks back her time making the series and the challenges she faced when taking on the key role of Chris Guthrie one last time, over ten years since she was first cast in the part in Sunset Song.
