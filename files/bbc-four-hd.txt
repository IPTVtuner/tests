The Vienna Philharmonic present a star-studded evening in celebration of the 150th anniversary of the world-famous opera house Palais Garnier in Paris. Featuring some of the biggest names in opera - bass-baritone Sir Bryn Terfel, tenors Juan Diego Flórez and Rolando Villazón, and soprano Sonya Yoncheva - this special concert is full of classical treats, with music from Bizet's Carmen, Verdi's La Traviata and more.
