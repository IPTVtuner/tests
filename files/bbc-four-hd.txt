Today, October 27
Paul Weller performs numbers from his double album `22 Dreams', hits from throughout his solo career including `From the Floorboards Up' and `Peacock Suit', plus a couple of classics from The Jam's back catalogue. Weller performs with his regular five-piece band and is joined on some numbers by The Wired Strings and a brass section. Special guests include Oasis guitarist Gem Archer and fiddle player Eliza Carthy.
