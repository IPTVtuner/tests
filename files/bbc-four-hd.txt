Today, December 22
Christine Lampard hosts a special gala concert to mark 100 years of the BBC in Northern Ireland. Recorded in the Waterfront Hall, Belfast, the show features performances from Clannad, Tony Hadley, D:Ream and Katharine Timoney with the Ulster Orchestra. The event reflects the range of BBC Northern Ireland's output and its role in community life, with support from Belfast City Council as part of its Belfast 2024 programme.
