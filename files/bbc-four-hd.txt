In 1978, `Top of the Pops' began to turn the credibility corner. It seemed the kids' time had come again for the first time since glam rock, yet the biggest-selling singles of 1978 were by the likes of Boney M, John Travolta and Olivia Newton John, Rod Stewart, the Bee Gees and Abba. Along came a generation of young post-punk and new wave bands ready to storm the `Top of the Pops' stage.
