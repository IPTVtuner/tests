Compilation of BBC archive performances by country artists over the last four decades, including Tammy Wynette, Kris Kristofferson, Johnny Cash and Dolly Parton. All the greats have performed for the BBC at some point - on entertainment shows, in concert and at the BBC studios. Some of the rhinestones revealed are Charley Pride's Crystal handeliers from the Lulu Show and Emmylou Harris singing `Together Again'.
