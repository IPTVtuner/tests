Alison Steadman talks about her impressive career, featuring archive footage and testimony from friends and colleagues. The programme progresses from her earliest appearances on screen, to what drove her choices, for good or ill, and their consequences. Contains footage and stills from programmes such as `Gavin and Stacey, Abigail's Party, Newshounds, Nuts in May' and her early appearances in `Frost'.
