The behind-the-scenes story of Felix Baumgarter's historic, record-breaking freefall from the edge of space to Earth. The world watched with bated breath when Felix became the first person to freefall through the sound barrier on the 15th October 2012, after jumping from 128,100ft (24 miles). The programme features footage from cameras attached to Felix as he achieved his magnificent feat.
