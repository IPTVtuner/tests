All the key moments from Bristol, where England take on the West Indies for the second T20 game of a three-match series. The World Cup is just seven months away, so this is a chance for both sides to test out their line-ups, with Harry Brook skippering England in a T20 series for the first time. Former captain Jos Buttler is also in the squad, which includes Hampshire all-rounder Liam Dawson, who has earned a recall for the first time since 2022.
