The 2016 Proms season draws to a close with Verdi's Requiem. Marin Alsop is at the helm, conducting the Orchestra of the Age of Enlightenment and the BBC Proms Youth Choir. Tom Service introduces for television and talks to Marin Alsop backstage. The Requiem had its British premiere in 1875 at the Royal Albert Hall with the composer himself conducting.
