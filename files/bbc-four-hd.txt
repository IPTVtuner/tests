First performed 150 years ago, Verdi's masterpiece roars into the Royal Albert Hall for its anniversary celebrations, as the BBC National Orchestra and Chorus of Wales, Crouch End Festival Chorus, and Ryan Bancroft perform 'Requiem'. The line-up of soloists features soprano Latonia Moore, mezzo-soprano Karen Cargill, tenor SeokJong Baek, bass Soloman Howard, and conductor Ryan Bancroft.
