Fiona Bruce uncovers the story of Leonardo da Vinci, who is considered by many to be one of the greatest artists who ever lived. Yet his reputation rests on only a handful of pictures - including the world's most famous painting, the `Mona Lisa'. Fiona travels to Florence, Milan, Paris and Warsaw to learn more about this enigmatic genius - and to New York, where she's given a preview of a new Leonardo work.
