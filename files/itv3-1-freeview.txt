Drama series which looks at aspects of life in a fashionable London household at the turn of the 20th century from the point of view of both the masters and the servants. It's Summer 1908 and Elizabeth Bellamy and her poet husband Lawrence have returned to Greenwich from their honeymoon. However, much to her distress, Elizabeth's first taste of married life is by no means sweet.
