The Squirrels stumble upon a room full of old radio equipment and discover that Duggee used to host his own show! Duggee reminisces about his radio days running Clubhouse Radio, playing music, telling jokes, chatting to callers and reading the news. It was the most popular show on air, until a new rival show appeared and replaced him: the tellingly-named Cheese Radio.
