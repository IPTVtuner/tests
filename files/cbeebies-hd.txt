Ranger Hamza and the Ramblers discover how earthworms play such an important role in the environment. They find out that earthworms keep the soil healthy by digging through it and eating things like dead leaves and twigs, which helps return nutrients to the soil. They learn that earthworms can be food for other animals too. On the way, they dig for worms before watering the plants with nutritious worm juice.
