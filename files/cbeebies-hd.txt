Andy travels back in time 220 million years, the time of Coelophysis - one of the earliest dinosaurs that hunted in packs and was an expert at fishing. His mission, to find a fish to replace the one that was eaten by Taffy the cat back at the museum. Along the way he witnesses Pterosaurs fishing as well as discovering that the fish he's after buries itself in the ground when the river dries up. But, will Andy succeed in time before the museum opens?
