A celebration of some of the best comedy from one of the greatest comedians of the last 50 years. Sir Billy Connolly started out as a folk singer and soon made his name as one of the funniest comedians of his generation, loved around the world for his tall tales, outrageous observations and side-splitting shaggy dog stories. Includes stand-up routines from some of his most critically acclaimed live shows and TV recordings in London and New York.
