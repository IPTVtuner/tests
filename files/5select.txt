Dr Jason Payne-James investigates the events leading up to the death of Nirvana frontman, Kurt Cobain. In April 1994, the 27-year-old musician and self-styled prince of grunge, Kurt Cobain, was found dead at his Seattle mansion with a gunshot wound to his head. A suicide note was found at the scene, and the police quickly concluded that Kurt had taken his own life.
