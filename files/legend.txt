Eddie Jillette is a cop with a vow to avenge his partner's death. He is drawn into a torrid affair with the only witness, the murderer's girlfriend. Cajun beauty Michel is caught in the war between Eddie and vicious crime lord Losado, the man who possess her. Jillette's vendetta takes him from the streets of Chicago to the violent underworld of New Orleans where he abducts Michel to flush out Losado.
