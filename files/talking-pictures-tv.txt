Dowdy housewife Ethel Gibbons is married to Frank, who has just returned from the war. Ethel had been sharing a house with her daughter Queenie, son Reg, crotchety mother Mrs Flint and spinster sister-in-law Sylvia. Frank returns from the war to move them all into a bigger house in Clapham and the story traces the ups and downs of the family's fortunes as the new peace allows them to reach for a greater standing in life.
