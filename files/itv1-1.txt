The UK is facing what has been called a cost of dying crisis as reports show that the average overall cost involved when someone dies - including professional fees, a basic funeral service and a party or wake - has reached a record high of 9,658 pounds. Tonight examines the rising costs of funerals and finds out how we can plan for the funeral that we want for ourselves or our loved ones while keeping an eye on how much we spend.
