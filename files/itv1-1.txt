Companion documentary to the drama series `Nolly' - narrated by Helena Bonham-Carter, who plays Nolly in the drama - explores the life of Noele Gordon. A star on the stage in the 1940s, Noele was also the first woman ever to be broadcast in colour and, in a world run by men, became a television executive who pioneered daytime TV.
