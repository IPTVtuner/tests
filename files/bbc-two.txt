Today, December 31
BBC wildlife presenters look back at their adventures across India, Bhutan and Bangladesh. Liz Bonnin remembers a remarkable morning migration by thousands of cranes to the small village of Khichan in Rajasthan. Colin Stafford-Johnson recounts his time filming tigers in India and forming a special relationship with the boisterous cub Brokentail. Chris Packham looks back on a tiger-spotting trip to Bangladesh, which lead to `one of the worst views of an animal he'd ever had'.
