Today, November 30
Paul Martin is joined by experts James Lewis and Christina Trevanion at the historic Imperial War Museum Duxford in Cambridgeshire. Together the team pick out a selection of antiques and collectibles to be sold at a auction. Among the aeronautically-themed treasures are some WWI medals and the prototype for the modern calculator. Paul also learns of the Hawker Hurricane contribution to the success of the Battle of Britain.
