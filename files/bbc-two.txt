Top criminal barristers Jeremy Dein QC and Sasha Wass QC reinvestigate a complex case from Victorian Britain that featured four prime suspects, two trials and ultimately just one man hanged for the murder of his neighbour. Norfolk, 1882. During the early hours of 19 November, in the narrow rows of Great Yarmouth, police on the beat noticed the door of one of the street's shops ajar. They searched the property and made a shocking discovery.
