BBC Two
Today, April 21
Coverage of the 44th London Marathon continues, including interviews with some of the inspirational people and famous faces competing in the race, many of whom are running to raise money for causes close to their hearts. Presenter Gabby Logan is joined by Steve Cram, Andrew Cotter, Baroness Grey-Thompson and Paula Radcliffe in the commentary box, while Jeanette Kwakye, JJ Chalmers and Steve Brown provide updates from around the course.
