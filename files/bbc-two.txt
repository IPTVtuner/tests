Hot from her triumph at the 2007 Brits where she won Best British Female in a tough field, Britain's best-selling albums artist of 2007, Amy Winehouse sashays into Bayswater's illustrious Porchester Hall for a one-off concert featuring songs from her current hit album, `Back to Black', including singles `Rehab' and `You Know I'm No Good', and 2003's Mercury Music Prize-nominated album, `Frank'.
