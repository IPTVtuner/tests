Christine McVie is undoubtedly the longest serving female band member of any of the enduring rock and roll acts that emerged from the 1960s. While she has never fronted Fleetwood Mac, Christine is their most successful singer-songwriter whose hits include `Over my Head', `Don't Stop' and `Everywhere'. Here, she is able to take centre stage and tell both her story and the saga of Fleetwood Mac.
