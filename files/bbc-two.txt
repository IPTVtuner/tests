Michael Portillo continues his exploration of Hong Kong aboard the impressive MTR metro system, which handles 6 million trips a day. In Wan Chai, he discovers why Hong Kongers are protesting all over the city and asks them about their hopes and fears. At Hong Kong University, he learns about the Father of Modern China, Sun Yat Sen, who was a student there at the time of his Bradshaw's Guide.
