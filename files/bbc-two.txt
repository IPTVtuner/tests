Today, June 20
Clare Balding presents all the top action from the Queen's Club in London as the men's singles second round comes to a close. The 2023 tournament saw USA's Taylor Fritz knocked out by Adrian Mannarino of France, while eventual champion Carlos Alcaraz beat Jiri Lehecka. Spain have had five victories in the men's singles title courtesy of Andres Gimeno, Feliciano Lopez and Rafael Nadal in addition to Alcaraz.
