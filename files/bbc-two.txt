Two more track cycling titles are decided in events that provide exciting and unpredictable spectacles: women's keirin and men's omnium. The latter concludes with the points race as Britain's two-time omnium world champion Ethan Hayter goes for gold. Emma Finucane was Britain's medal hopeful in the keirin, a six-lap event in which riders battle it out over a sprint finish.
