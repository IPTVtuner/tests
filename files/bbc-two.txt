BBC Two
Today, May 16
Live coverage as the biggest stars in world athletics descend on Doha for the third Diamond League meeting of 2025. A raft of Olympic champions are due to take part including in the high jump, where reigning gold medallist Hamish Kerr is set to resume his rivalry with former Olympic winner Mutaz Barshim. Elsewhere, Great Britain's 2024 world indoor champion Molly Caudery takes part in the pole vault, facing off against two-time world champion Katie Moon and Canadian record holder Alysha Newman.
