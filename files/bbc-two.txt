Former speaker of the House of Commons John Bercow takes the wheel on this road trip with athletics superstar Sally Gunnell. Along for the ride in the Rolls Royce Corniche are experts Izzy Balmer and Raj Bisra as the teams battle around Brighton in search of bargains.
