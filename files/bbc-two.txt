The live cricket action from the Hundred continues as the Northern Superchargers men's team host London Spirit at Headingley in Leeds. These two finished as the bottom two sides in 2023's competition, with the Superchargers propping up the table after registering just two wins from their eight matches. However, the appointment of cricketing legend Andrew Flintoff as their new coach has given the team renewed optimism ahead of the 2024 campaign.
