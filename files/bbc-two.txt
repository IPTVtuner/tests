Today's episode, with Charlie Ross, comes from the Bath & West Showground near Shepton Mallet. A married couple face off with a team of good friends! Experts Philip Serrell and Tim Weeks are on hand to help the reds and the blues buy the most profitable three items to take to auction. Charlie learns about art in miniature and meets a second-generation miniaturist with a passion for painting nautical scenes.
