Today, December 16
Live coverage of the draw for the Women's Euro 2025 finals from Lausanne in host country Switzerland. Holders England are one of 16 nations poised to discover their fate ahead of 2025's tournament, with Sarina Wiegman's side drawn in Pot 2 despite being reigning champions. The Lionesses made history at their home tournament in 2022, with a dramatic extra-time victory against Germany in the final at Wembley that sealed their first major trophy in front of a record crowd of 87,192.
