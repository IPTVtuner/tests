After losing her father, Cheryl Castling was determined to live life to the fullest, and thought that a move to Oz could be the adventure she and her husband Adam needed. Adam was less than convinced, despite being the one to have the idea in the first place. Could a week of living the Aussie life make him see where Cheryl was coming from? Four years on, have they been able to come to a decision about where home should be?
