Today, October 27
Revenge is firmly on the agenda as England attempt to erase the heartache of their agonising World Cup semi-final defeat when they resume hostilities with Samoa. England were huge favourites when the two nations met in the last four of the Rugby League World Cup two years ago, having won the group game against Samoa by 54 points. It was the Pacific Islanders, however, who had the last laugh, shocking the tournament hosts in golden-point extra-time to reach a maiden World Cup final.
