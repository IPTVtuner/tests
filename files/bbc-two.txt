Today, June 08
Over a decade after the Lockerbie bombing, following much debate and international negotiation, the accused will finally stand trial. The venue will be a purpose-built Scottish court in the Netherlands. Bereaved relatives around the world campaign for access to the trial. Scottish police and the FBI continue to work tirelessly to secure vital witness testimony and deliver meticulously collected forensic evidence. Will the investigators finally secure justice for the victims and their families?
