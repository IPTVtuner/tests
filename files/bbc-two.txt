Stan and Andrea Baum had their lives turned upside down when Stan was involved in a motorcycle accident that nearly cost him his life. Andrea believed that a move to Australia would help with his rehabilitation and recovery, but both were unsure of saying goodbye to the support network that had got them through such a hard time in their lives. A week in Brisbane helped them decide whether Australia was the place for them - but 6 years since we last saw them, where in the world do they call home?
