It's a final pairing few would have predicted at the start of the competition as Cork play Munster rivals Clare in the All-Ireland Hurling final. The sides last met in an All-Ireland final back in 2013, with Clare eventually getting the better of Cork after a replay. Both sides went into their perspective semi-finals as underdogs and came out victorious, Cork having beat Limerick, ending their drive for five consecutive All-Ireland titles.
