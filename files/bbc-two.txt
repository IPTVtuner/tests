Ainsley Harriott has picked dishes where the combination of ingredients initially seem a little unlikely, until you see the results. The menu includes Michel Roux Jr's beef cheeks with pears garlic and chocolate, Nigel Slater's cheesy apple pie, Rachel Khoo's figs and chicken livers and a `Bake Off' masterclass that combines blackcurrant and liquorice.
