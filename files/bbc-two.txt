Sir Tom Jones wows at Worthy Farm in the legendary teatime slot, Sunday, 28 June 2009, on the Pyramid Stage. Sporting his recent silver fox look and with a full band, Tom delivers rousing versions of all his biggest hits, including 'Delilah', 'It's Not Unusual', 'What's New Pussycat?', 'Kiss', 'Sex Bomb', and a cover of EMF's 'Unbelievable', while the crowd sing along and party in the sunshine.
