Highlights from Antalya, Turkey, as the finest cross country runners on the continent face off for championship glory across the sand and mud of Dokuma Park. Great Britain topped the medal table again with seven gold medals in 2023, including individual victories for Innes FitzGerald and Will Barnicoat in the women's under-20 and men's under-23 races respectively. Both athletes are eligible to defend their titles.
