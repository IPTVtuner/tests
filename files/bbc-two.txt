The story of Brian Clough's 44 day stint as manager of Leeds United Football Club. England, in 1974. When Don Revie quits Leeds to become the England boss, the outspoken Brian Clough takes charge. Determined to impose his own style upon Revie's tough tackling team, Clough soon alienates his players and the board. Based on the book by David Pearce.
