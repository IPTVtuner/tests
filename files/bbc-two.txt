Today, October 19
Live coverage from the penultimate day of action at the Track Cycling World Championships in Ballerup, Denmark. Emma Finucane was one of Great Britain's stars of the 2024 Olympics, claiming three medals, one of which was time trial bronze. However, Ellesse Andrews of New Zealand was the golden girl in Paris as she stormed to gold. Jill Douglas presents, with commentary coming from Simon Brotherton and Chris Boardman.
