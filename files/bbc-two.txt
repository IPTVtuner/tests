Kelly Somers introduces football's annual dose of anticipation and nostalgia as lower and non-league sides keep everything crossed for a dream draw against exalted opposition. The FA Cup third round is when the Premier League clubs enter the competition, including holders Manchester United. The Red Devils were worthy winners over neighbours Manchester City in the 2023/24 season's final, but this round is the first involvement in the world's oldest football competition for Ruben Amorim.
