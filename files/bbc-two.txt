There's a saying that 'where there's muck there's brass.' In today's show, Danny Sebastian meets a farmer and collector of horse brass who has an incredible 5,000 pieces! Meanwhile, two teams try to earn some brass of their own by hunting for three profitable items at Builth Wells antiques fair in mid-Wales. Skippering them are experts Kate Bliss and Izzie Balmer.
