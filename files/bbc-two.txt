Today, November 17
Andrew Cotter introduces highlights as the leading golfer on the European Tour is crowned at the season-ending DP World Tour Championship in Dubai. The tournament marks the climax of the Race to Dubai, which celebrates the tour's number-one ranked player. Past winners include Rory McIlroy, Matt Fitzpatrick and Jon Rahm, while Denmark's Nicolai Hojgaard is the defending champion.
