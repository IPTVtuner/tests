The 1985 Black Ball Final made snooker legend Dennis Taylor a household name, and there's nowhere that celebrates this giant of sport more than his native Tyrone. Anna visits him at the snooker hall where it all began. Dennis can still remember the soda bread his grandmother used to make over an open fire. Can Anna recreate this childhood recipe?The pair then join fishermen on the largest lake in the UK to celebrate one of Ireland's most unique delicacies - the Lough Neagh eel.
