BBC Two
Today, May 05
Mark Chapman introduces highlights of the day's three Premier League games, which include a clash between long-standing rivals Chelsea and Liverpool at Stamford Bridge. These sides have had contrasting seasons under two bosses who are in their debut campaigns in charge of a Premier League club. While Chelsea's form under Enzo Maresca has been up and down, Arne Slot has led Liverpool on an unstoppable march towards a record-equalling 20th top-flight title.
