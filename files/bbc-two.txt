Weatherman Derek Brockway participates in two walks close to the English border. In the first walk, he takes to the hills overlooking Abergavenny to scale the Skirrid. He then heads to Holywell to take a dip in St Winifride's Well before travelling down the Greenfield Valley to the coastal path along the Dee estuary and finishing at Flint castle.
