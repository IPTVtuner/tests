Rugby league highlights from the penultimate round of the regular season, presented by Tanya Arnold. The race for the League Leaders' Shield has been closer than ever with Wigan, Catalans and St Helens battling it out to finish in first place. This week Wigan are at home to Castleford, Catalans host Leeds and St Helens travel to Warrington. Hull FC face Huddersfield and there's a crucial match in the fight to secure a play-off place between Hull KR and Salford.
