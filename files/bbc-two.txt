BBC Two
Today, November 05
Coverage as League One Charlton host near neighbours Cray Valley Paper Mills of the eighth tier, with the part-timers seeking to pull off one of the shocks of the season. Charlton Athletic have received a home draw against non-league opposition for the third successive season, and the Addicks comfortably progressed from the previous two ties, beating Havant & Waterlooville 4-0 in 2021-22 and Coalville Town 4-1 last year.
