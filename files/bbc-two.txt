BBC Two
Today, October 08
Live coverage from the final day of the 2023 Artistic Gymnastics Championships as the best gymnasts in the world contest the remaining apparatus finals in Antwerp. British superstar Jessica Gadirova is seeking to defend the individual floor title that she won in Liverpool last year, which saw her become the second British woman to win the event, after Beth Tweddle in 2009. Fellow Brit Jake Jarman is also in action and is looking to add to his Commonwealth and European titles in the men's vault.
