Today, September 26
Tanya Arnold presents highlights from the final full round of matches in the 2023 Super League season. The battle to finish in first place reaches its conclusion, with Wigan, Catalans and St Helens all having a chance to lift the League Leaders Shield and claim pole position in the play-offs. Wigan make the short journey to Leigh, St Helens are at home to Hull FC whilst Catalans travel to Salford.
