Today, September 09
Live coverage from Belgrade, where Britain's rowers will be trying to win world titles and book places for next year's Paris Olympics. After a disappointing Games in Tokyo two years ago, where Team GB failed to win a rowing gold for the first time since 1980, British rowing is looking in rude health once again. A post-Tokyo squad overhaul at both athlete and coaching level is seemingly paying dividends, with Britain crowned 2023 World Rowing Cup champions in July.
