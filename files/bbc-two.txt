Margherita Taylor and Matt Baker head to the Trent Valley, where over the past two centuries, industrialisation along the UK's third largest river - the river Trent - has taken its toll on the landscape. Mining, agriculture and increased development have all lead to the loss of vital habitats. However, with the conclusion of a major transformation project, the area is now recovering and welcoming back wildlife to the area.
