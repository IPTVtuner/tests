It's Hugh's belief that if making and baking your own homemade bread isn't a part of your weekly kitchen repertoire it should be. In this show, he makes everything from simple white loaves to tangy sourdoughs and cheese and Guinness stuffed soda breads. There are also tips on how to turn leftovers into tasty treats, from an Italian bread salad to a three coursed breadcrumb dinner.
