Jamie Oliver brings together his all-time classic Christmas recipes brought to life with animation. This first programme features all the recipes viewers need for the perfect, stress-free Christmas Day: simple and succulent turkey, tasty stuffing, golden roasties, luscious gravy and all the veg. There are puds galore too, including a winter pudding bombe, DIY chocolate truffles and - of course - mince pies.
