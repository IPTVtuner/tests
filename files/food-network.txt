Nadiya has just spent a week with her family in Sylhet, and in this episode she is setting off on her own to cook her way around Bangladesh and learn more about the country, its people and its food. In the capital city Dhaka, Nadiya spends time with a charity that delivers school dinners to some of the country's most deprived children, and she cooks coconut and fennel samosas as a delicious treat for the kids.
