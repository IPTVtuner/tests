Dave Ja Vu
Today, November 22
Rob Brydon, Lee Mack and David Mitchell return for previously unseen material, and a stellar cast of celebrity guests reveal amazing stories about themselves. But are they telling the truth, or are they making it all up? Among the guests joining Lee Mack and David Mitchell are James Acaster, David Baddiel, Ed Balls, Mark Bonnar, Jo Brand, Steve Davis, Susie Dent, Ade Edmondson, Sheila Hancock, Claude Littner, Cariad Lloyd, Gabby Logan, Stephen Mangan, Bob Mortimer, Melvin Odoom, and Ore Oduba.
