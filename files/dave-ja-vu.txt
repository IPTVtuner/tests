Today, May 10
Angus Deayton and Mariella Frostrup drive up Madagascar's eastern coast during the tail end of the rainy season. This is a route with countless river crossings, offering Angus and Mariella a terrifying array of broken bridges and flimsy ferries as their only way across. The roads themselves are mired with thick mud, making progress increasingly painful and even forcing the pair to spend a night the car.
