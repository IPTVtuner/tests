Dave Ja Vu
Today, May 21
Rob Brydon, Lee Mack and David Mitchell return in an episode packed with some of the best bits from series 14. Over the course of the show, a stellar cast of celebrity guests reveal amazing stories about themselves. But are they telling the truth, or are they making it all up? Among the guests joining Lee and David are Richard Osman, Claudia Winkleman, Samantha Morton, AJ Odudu, Miles Jupp, Laura Whitmore, Lou Sanders, Chris McCausland, Sara Barron, Raj Bisram, Bob Mortimer and Sara Pascoe.
