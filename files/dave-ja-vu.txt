Dave Ja Vu
Today, January 07
Gino D'Acampo returns with another series exploring the culinary treasures of his homeland, Italy. This time the chef travels off the beaten track to discover local traditions, culture and stunning landscapes. Over the episodes he visits seven regions beginning in Abruzzo, before moving North to Trentino Alto-Adige and Lombardia, then through to Umbria and Tuscany, up to Liguria and ending in Piemonte. Along the way D'Acampo shares his top cooking tips and creates recipes inspired by his journey that viewers can try at home.
