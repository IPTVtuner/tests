Today, November 02
Hard-hitting rugby league action continues from Headingley Stadium in Leeds as England welcome their World Cup semi-final conquerors Samoa in the second of their two-Test series. Shaun Wane's side were heavy favourites to set up a final against Australia when hosting the World Cup two years ago but were left shocked by the Samoans, who produced an astonishing result in London to win in golden-point extra-time and reach a maiden World Cup final.
