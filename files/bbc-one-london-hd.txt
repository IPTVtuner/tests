Romesh Ranganathan is back at the helm of one of the world's most iconic quizzes. Eight celebrities initially work together to bank as much money as possible before voting off the weakest link in each round. Following a final head-to-head, one player will take home the prize money for their chosen charity. The celebrity contestants competing to be the strongest link are Stacey Dooley, Adrian Chiles, Josie Gibson, Rufus Hound, GK Barry, Guz Khan, Sophie Duker and Mark Lane.
