Today, April 20
Gary Lineker hosts live coverage of the FA Cup semi-final between holders Manchester City and eight-time winners Chelsea at Wembley, with a place in the 143rd FA Cup final at stake. The two sides met at this stage in 2021, with Thomas Tuchel's Chelsea reaching the final after a 1-0 victory over Pep Guardiola's side. In fact, the Blues have made it to five of the last seven finals in this famous competition.
