Nicki Chapman is in East Devon helping an outdoors-loving couple armed with 850,000 pounds, who are ready to leave their hectic life in St Albans. They are searching for a rural family home within reach of the coast before their son starts school. Along the way, Nicki drops in on a boat building family in Beer, who for over 70 years have been making and restoring traditional vessels designed and built for Devon's shores.
