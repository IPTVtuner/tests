Continued live coverage of the apparatus finals from the penultimate day of the 2023 Artistic Gymnastics Championships in Antwerp. American superstar Simone Biles is one of the many big names on show in Belgium, and she is seeking yet another gold medal in the vault final. The 26 year-old is the most decorated female gymnast in world championship history, and despite taking a two-year break from the sport, she has accrued 25 medals, including 19 golds, prior to this event.
