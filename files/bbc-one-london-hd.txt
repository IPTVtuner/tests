BBC ONE London HD
Today, May 25
Live coverage of the FA Cup final between holders Manchester City and twelve-time winners Manchester United at Wembley. This is the first time since 1885 that the FA Cup final will be contested by the same two clubs in consecutive years. City were victorious 12 months ago, thanks to two goals from captain Ilkay Gundogan, either side of a penalty converted by United captain Bruno Fernandes.
