The roadshow travels to Beaumaris Castle on the island of Anglesey, north Wales, where jewellery expert Joanna Hardy is dazzled by a rare ruby and diamond collection worn by a family for generations of weddings. Lisa Lloyd is fascinated by a wooden figurine hidden in a secret cupboard and identifies it as a ceremonial African puppet. Ben Rogers Jones sees a collection of photographs and ephemera relating to one of Wales's most important historical figures, Prime Minister David Lloyd George.
