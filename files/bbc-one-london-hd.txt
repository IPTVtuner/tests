Nigel Havers welcomes sellers of collectibles and curiosities to the Bidding Room's home in Edinburgh, where they to ply their wares to five dealers. First to march her way into the room is Donna from Essex, with a military campaign tub with a lockable lid. Dating back to the 1890s, it's been stored in Donna's family loft for years, and the time has come to send it on its next adventure amongst the dealers.
