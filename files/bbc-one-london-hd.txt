In this music spectacular from Edinburgh Castle, Tattoo performers including the Massed Pipes and Drums are joined by Swiss drummers Majesticks, Indian bhangra dancers from Teamwork Arts and the US Citadel Regimental Band and Pipes. There is also a navy-led cast of HM Royal Marines Bands, the US Navy Ceremonial Guard Drill Team and the US Navy Sea Services Band.
