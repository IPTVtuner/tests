Today, August 03
Isa Guha and Mark Chapman bring you all the headlines from the busiest day of the Olympics so far, with a plethora of star names in action as 27 gold medals were decided. The women's 100m final was the day's showpiece event, with Great Britain poster girl Dina Asher-Smith hoping to be in the mix against America's Sha'Carri Richardson and Jamaican Shelly-Ann Fraser-Pryce, plus legendary British gymnast Max Whitlock was seeking a fourth successive pommel horse medal.
