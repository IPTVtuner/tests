Highlights from the penultimate round of fixtures in the league stage of the Champions League, with Liverpool heading the teams aiming to book their place in the last 16. The Reds, who are the only team with a 100 per cent record in the revamped competition, welcome Lille to Anfield. Premier League champions Manchester City are also up against French opposition, with a tricky visit to Paris to face PSG. Arsenal welcome Dinamo Zagreb to the Emirates Stadium.
