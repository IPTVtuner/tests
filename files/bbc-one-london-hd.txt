This year's star-studded BAFTA Television Awards are once again hosted by comedians and friends Rob Beckett and Romesh Ranganathan. The biggest night in the British TV calendar will see stars from The Crown, Happy Valley and Succession flock to the Royal Festival Hall in the hope of taking home a coveted BAFTA mask. Viewers will also be treated to exclusive performances from platinum-selling artist Ella Eyre and world-renowned dance company Rambert.
