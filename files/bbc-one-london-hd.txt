Live coverage as League Two Salford City aim to cause a massive upset against reigning Premier League champions Manchester City in the FA Cup third round. City have lifted the famous trophy seven times, reaching the final on a further six occasions, including last season. Their only FA Cup defeat to a lower league side under Pep Guardiola was a shock 1-0 loss to Wigan in 2018. Salford City were promoted to the Football League in 2019, aided by investment from former Manchester United stars.
