Today, March 10
In the presence of HM the King and senior members of the royal family, Reeta Chakrabarti introduces live coverage from Westminster Abbey in London of the annual Service of Celebration for Commonwealth Day. The multicultural service features music, poetry, dance and readings drawn from across the globe, including special performances by Joan Armatrading and members of the Kanneh-Mason family among others. Sonali Shah interviews special guests and those participating in the service.
