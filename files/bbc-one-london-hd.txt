Today, December 25
The traditional Christmas Eve service and Blessing of the Crib is broadcast live from the Collegiate Church of St Mary's, Warwick. The service is introduced and led by the Rev Canon Angus Aagaard, rector of St Mary's, with a sermon by the Rt Rev Martin Gordon, bishop of Goma, visiting from the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Music is led by director of music Oliver Hancock and the St Mary's Choir.
