Join art critic Alastair Sooke on a journey of discovery to the world's most inspiring museums. This time he's in Doha, capital of the Gulf state of Qatar, to explore the Museum of Islamic Art. The collection tells the story of the blossoming of Islamic art in the region and beyond; influencing and cross-fertilising with diverse cultures and traditions in places as far afield as Spain and Indonesia.
