This episode is a Duos Special, featuring eight contestants formed into four pairs of celebrities. They must work together as a team. Will the duos work together, or as the pressure mounts, will it be every player for themselves? Contestants in this episode are DJ Tyler West, actor Molly Rainford, Mollie Pearce and Harry Clark from The Traitors, ex-Goggleboxers Stephen and Daniel Lustig-Webb, and The Repair Shop's Suzie Fletcher and Brenton West.
