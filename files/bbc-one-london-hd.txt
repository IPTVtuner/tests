Live coverage of the semi-finals from Queen's, where a stellar line-up has gathered to take part in the first women's event at the famous venue for more than half a century. Reigning Wimbledon champion Barbora Krejcikova, former winners Petra Kvitova and Elena Rybakina, and quadruple Grand Slam champion Naomi Osaka were among those hoping to reach the last four, along with 2021 US Open champion Emma Raducanu and British number one Katie Boulter.
