Can a chance encounter with a stray Spanish dog and a bag of crisps lead to an epiphany and a fresh approach to life? Of course it can't, but is that a reason not to build an entire show around it? This show is the first to ever attempt to chronicle an entire life without offering even the slightest insight into the human condition, every significant incident forensically removed to leave only the lean meat of the trivial.
