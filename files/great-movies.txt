An adaptation of the novel by Stieg Larsson. Mikael Blomkvist is a disgraced journalist who is offered the chance to write a family biography for Henry Vanger, a wealthy industrialist, and to secretly find out what happened to Harriet Vanger who went missing 40 years ago. He is joined by Lisbeth Salander, who is a computer hacker.
