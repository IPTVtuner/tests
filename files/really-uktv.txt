Designer Jacqui Joseph is at Earlswood recycling centre in Surrey, hoping to swoop in on quality items before they're discarded forever. An old ottoman, some rusted garden gates and unwanted printer trays all catch her attention, and with the help of upholsterer Sharon O'Connor and blacksmith Kev Paxton, Jacqui sets to work transforming the pieces into profit makers.
