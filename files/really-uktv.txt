Sarah Moore is at Keynsham Recycling Centre in Somerset, hoping to unearth some quality discards before they hit the rubbish heap. With the help of upholsterer Simion Hawtin-Smith and carpentry duo Josh Kennard and Oli Milne, Sarah hopes to breathe new life into some redundant rolls of fabric, a rusty filing cabinet and a single dining chair. But will she produce any profits by selling them on?
