First to test the Repair Shop's technical prowess, Julianna Smith from Milton Keynes. She is hoping Mark Stuckey's mastery of electronics can bring a radio system, handmade by her late father, back to life. Julianna remembers the radiogram filling the house with rock and roll music as a child. Her dear father, Patrick, was a clever chap and this creation, built from scratch, was his pride and joy. With no instruction manual for this unique item, Mark takes a slow and steady approach.
