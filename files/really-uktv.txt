In the West Midlands, Jacqui Joseph scours Walsall Recycling Centre for quality items. Her mission is to save three things, have them transformed or revitalised and try to sell them for a profit. She has her sights set on a chest of drawers, there's a much-loved set of six dining chairs for upholsterer Leigh-Anne Treadwell to work on, and signwriter Joel Poole works his magic on a mid-century cabinet.
