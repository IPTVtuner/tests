Today, September 15
The Roadshow returns to Pitzhanger Manor and Gallery in west London, where finds include a unique engagement ring, a note written by Oscar Wilde and an Olympic medal from the 1930s. Ronnie Archer-Morgan finds beautiful jewellery made by the Navajo people from the arid south west of the United States, while Fuchsia Voremberg hears the story of one man's life-changing journey to the icy wastes of Antarctica.
