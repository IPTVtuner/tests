BBC One London
Today, August 05
Hazel Irvine presents live coverage as an unmissable women's floor final reaches its climax in the artistic gymnastics competition and British paddlers go for gold in the chaotic kayak cross. Great Britain boast current men's and women's world champions Joe Clarke and Kimberley Woods in kayak cross, a new addition to the canoe slalom Olympics programme, in which competitors go head-to-head on the white-water course.
