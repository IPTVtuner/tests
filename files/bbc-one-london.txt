BBC One London
Today, August 06
Jeanette Kwakye and JJ Chalmers present another busy morning in Paris, with the women's 1,500m, men's javelin and men's 400m hurdles all getting started at the Stade de France. Brilliant Kenyan Faith Kipyegon and Britain's Laura Muir will look to make safe passage in the 1,500m, while Olympic champions Neeraj Chopra and Karsten Warholm begin the defence of their titles in the javelin and 400m hurdles respectively.
