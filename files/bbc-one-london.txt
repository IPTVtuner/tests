BBC One London
Today, September 27
The race to Tromsø is underway. The four pairs must leave Pinhão, nestled in Portugal's Douro Valley, to navigate 2000km through Spain and France to the second checkpoint in Bonifacio, perched on the stunning island of Corsica. The competing teams are McFly drummer, Harry Judd, with his mum, Emma, British racing driver and pundit Billy Monger and his sister, Bonny, All Saints singer and songwriter, Mel Blatt, and her mum, Helene, and broadcaster and weatherman Alex Beresford and his dad, Noel.
