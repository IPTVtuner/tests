Michael McIntyre is back with his hit game show that's packed with huge stars, big laughs and a giant spinning wheel. This series has more celebrities than ever before, with the introduction of the 'Double Chair', as well as a higher stakes 'Cashout Question'. Can the contestants answer their way to a fortune? The celebrities on this episode are Jonathan Ross, Joanne McNally, Gethin Jones, Judi Love, Niko Omilana, Clodagh McKenna and William Hanson.
