BBC One London
Today, July 30
Join Clare Balding and guests as Team GB seek to defend their Olympic title in the men's 4x200m freestyle relay - one of three finals on another bumper night of swimming. The British men dominated this event in Tokyo, finishing an emphatic 3.23 seconds ahead of the field to take gold. This time, they will face stiff competition from the likes of the USA and Australia, as a gallery of stars line up for one of the best races in the swimming calendar.
