BBC One London
Today, August 07
Some of the biggest names in athletics will battle for the podium on a remarkable night of Olympic sport, with Cornwall's Molly Caudery among the British contenders. Caudery won the world indoor title in March and broke the national record in June. Can she continue that form to earn an Olympic medal? Fellow Brit Matt Hudson-Smith has developed a major medal-winning habit in recent years and will expect to be in the mix again in the 400m.
