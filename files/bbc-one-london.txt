Today, September 21
Gary Lineker presents highlights from a busy day of Premier League action with eight games taking place, including Manchester United's trip to the capital to face Crystal Palace. Erik ten Hag's side were beaten 4-0 at Selhurst Park last season, with the Eagles completing a league double over the Red Devils for the very first time. Other matches include a London derby between West Ham United and Chelsea, while Aston Villa host Wolverhampton Wanderers in an all-Midlands affair.
