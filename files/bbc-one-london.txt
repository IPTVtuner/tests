BBC One London
Today, August 18
Alex Scott presents highlights of the day's two Premier League matches, including the game between Chelsea and reigning champions Manchester City. This will be the Londoners' first competitive fixture under new boss Enzo Maresca, who replaced Mauricio Pochettino this summer. The Italian led Leicester City to the Championship title last season and previously worked as part of Pep Guardiola's coaching staff at Manchester City.
