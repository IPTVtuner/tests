Gary Lineker introduces highlights of the day's seven Premier League games, including reigning champions Manchester City at home to Fulham. Pep Guardiola's side have not lost to the Cottagers in the top flight since 2009 and won 5-1 in last season's corresponding fixture, with Erling Haaland scoring a hat trick. Arsenal host newly promoted Southampton, while Liverpool visit Crystal Palace.
