Today, November 20
Nigel Havers welcomes sellers of collectibles and curiosities to the Bidding Room's new home in Edinburgh, where they try to ply their wares to five dealers. First through the doors is apprentice gas engineer Dannii with a skip find that she thought was worth salvaging. Concealed inside her mysterious wooden box is a vintage chlorine tester - once used by the water board to test chlorine levels in drinking water. What price will the dealers put on this life-saving piece of equipment?
