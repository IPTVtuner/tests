The Earthshot Prize 2023 is a star-studded celebration of the very best ideas and initiatives to help save our planet. Attended by Earthshot founder, HRH Prince William, and Sir David Attenborough, and including special musical performances by OneRepublic and Bastille among others, this glamorous event, held in spectacular Singapore, will treat us to the inspirational stories of everyday heroes around the globe, who are succeeding in their ambitions to reduce our impact and improve our lives.
