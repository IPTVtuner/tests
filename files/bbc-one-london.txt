Ross Kemp hosts as a team of pop stars - Pussycat Doll Kimberly Wyatt, Claire Richards from Steps, Eternal's Kéllé Bryan and Sugababe Amelle Berrabah - take on the Bridge of Lies for their chosen charities. One by one, they face the bridge, which has 32 potential steps, including 22 truths and 10 lies. They must cross by stepping on the truths and avoiding the lies, earning money for each correct answer.
