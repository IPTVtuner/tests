BBC One London
Today, August 10
Join Hazel Irvine for what promises to be a breathless afternoon of Olympic action as more medals are decided in diving, track cycling, golf and rhythmic gymnastics. British hopes will be focused on the aquatics centre and velodrome, with diver Noah Williams hoping to challenge the imperious Chinese in the men's 10m platform final, while track cyclist Ethan Hayter will have a new partner in the madison as he seeks a second successive Olympic medal in the event.
