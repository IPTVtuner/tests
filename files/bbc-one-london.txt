BBC One London
Today, April 21
Gabby Logan presents live coverage of the 44th London Marathon as world-class runners join thousands of fundraisers on one of the most inspiring days in the British sporting calendar. This famous race always attracts a stellar field, and this year is no different, with world record holder Tigst Assefa and Kenya's Olympic champion Peres Jepchirchir among the star attractions in the women's elite race. The men's race is headlined by New York City Marathon champion Tamirat Tola.
