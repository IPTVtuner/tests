TM the King and Queen will be joined by members of the royal family at the Royal British Legion's annual Festival of Remembrance from the Royal Albert Hall in London, hosted by Adrian Lester. This year's event will celebrate the remarkable men and women who fought in World War II in 1944. To mark this 80th anniversary, we will commemorate those who fought on D-Day and in the Normandy campaign. We will also hear stories from those that fought in more recent conflicts.
