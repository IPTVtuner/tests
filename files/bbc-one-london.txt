BBC One London
Today, June 28
Dua Lipa, Grammy and BRIT Award-winning global superstar and purveyor of pop bangers, steps out as the first Pyramid Stage headliner of Glastonbury 2024. She returns to perform on Worthy Farm for the third time, having made her debut in 2016 swiftly followed in 2017 by a momentous set, which she refers to as a career highlight. Now she graces the Pyramid Stage in the top spot as one of the biggest artists in the world, fresh off the back of the release of her third album, Radical Optimism.
