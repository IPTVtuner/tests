BBC One London
Today, December 15
Romesh Ranganathan takes to the podium to host one of the world's most iconic quizzes. Eight celebrities hope to show off their general knowledge and bank as much money as possible for charity, before voting off The Weakest Link each round. After a final head-to-head, one player will be crowned the strongest link and take the prize. Celebrity contestants in this episode are Kimberly Wyatt, Ellie Taylor, Rhys James, Sinitta, Darren Harriott, Gregg Wallace, Hannah Byczkowski and Lesley Garrett.
