BBC One London
Today, June 15
Ross Kemp presents this high stakes quiz, where a team from BBC Morning Live featuring host Gethin Jones, baking expert Bryony May Williams, Dr Ranj Singh and chef Anna Haugh get together to take on the Bridge of Lies for their chosen charities. One by one, they all face the Bridge, choosing who faces each crossing depending on the question category. Each Bridge crossing has 32 potential steps, including 22 truths and ten lies.
