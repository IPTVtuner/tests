BBC One London
Today, January 31
Clive Myrie puts four more famous faces under the spotlight in another episode of the classic quiz, where celebrities donate to their chosen charities. Cricket broadcaster Jonathan Agnew, musician and presenter YolanDa Brown, actor Davood Ghadami and writer and comedian Jessica Knappett are in the famous black chair tonight, answering questions on the history of cakes, Disney's Descendants films, the Battle of Thermopylae and The Fast Show.
