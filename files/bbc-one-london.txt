Today, August 18
Antiques Roadshow is at Dyffryn Gardens near Cardiff, where treasures include a necklace made from some of the largest pieces of Baltic amber that jewellery specialist John Benjamin has ever seen. Jon Baddeley admires a humble compass once owned by local man Edgar Evans, who was part of Scott's ill-fated Antarctic expedition and one of the first of the party to lose his life on the ice. Frances Christie admires a vibrant flower painting by artist Bryan Organ.
