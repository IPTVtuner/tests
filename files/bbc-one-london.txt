Gabby Logan and Clare Balding host an electric evening of Olympic action as athletics and swimming share the spotlight, with Britain tipped to increase their medal haul in the pool. The much-anticipated men's 50m freestyle final kicks things off, with Great Britain's Ben Proud battling a host of big names for Olympic gold. That's followed by the women's 200m backstroke final featuring Scotland's Katie Shanahan and Australia's Kaylee McKeown, before a star-studded men's 200m individual medley.
