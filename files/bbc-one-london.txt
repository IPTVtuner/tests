Clive Myrie asks the questions in another celebrity episode of the classic quiz where celebrities donate to their chosen charities. Actress and singer Tricia Penrose, actor and TV presenter Karim Zeroual, stand-up comedian Catherine Bohart and BBC Sport presenter Gavin Ramjaun are in the famous black chair answering questions on Madonna in the 1980s, the films of Jim Carrey, Vita Sackville-West and the Mission: Impossible films.
