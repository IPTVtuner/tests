BBC One London
Today, November 09
Live coverage of one of the world's oldest ceremonial spectacles. The streets of London come alive with music, marching bands and entertainment as the City welcomes its new lord mayor of London. Mishal Husain sets the scene from the balcony of Mansion House, while Radzi Chinyanganya joins the celebrations on the parade route to meet some of the 7,000 people taking part.
