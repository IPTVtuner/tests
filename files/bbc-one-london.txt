BBC One London
Today, May 26
Live coverage of the Great Manchester Run as some of the world's best long-distance runners join tens of thousands of amateurs running for a host of good causes along the streets of Manchester. 2023 saw the men's elite race won by Italian Eyob Ghebrehiwet Faniel, while Kenyan Olympic 5,000m silver medallist Hellen Obiri won the women's race as Manchester celebrated 20 years of the event.
