Today, September 22
The Roadshow visits Thirlestane Castle in the Scottish Borders, where treasures include a Louis Vuitton packing case, a beautiful travelling clock, and medals awarded to one of the airmen involved in the Dambusters bombing raid. Susan Rumfitt is delighted to appraise a Scottish agate bangle, while Gordon Foster is intrigued to see a fighting knife that may date from the infamous massacre of Glencoe in 1692.
