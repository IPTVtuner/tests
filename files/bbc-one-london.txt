The roadshow pays a final visit to Alexandra Gardens in Cardiff, where treasures include a rare Welsh rugby shirt, colourful cameo glass and unseen photos of The Rolling Stones. Mark Hill is excited to see a poster and a collection of unpublished photos documenting one of The Rolling Stones' first concerts, while Joanna Hardy is delighted to see a repurposed piece of silver jewellery from India.
