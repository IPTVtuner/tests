The end of the year can be a time for gathering families together and for reflection,and in this festive special, the roadshow looks back on some of the most memorable, intriguing and moving stories to have appeared in recent years to find out: 'what happened next?' From photo slides of young boys about to be shipped to Canada at the turn of the 20th century to the story of a pioneering female pilot in World War II - the team learn what happened after the cameras stopped rolling.
