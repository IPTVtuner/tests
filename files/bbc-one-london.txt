Today, July 29
Hazel Irvine guides you through what could be a golden afternoon for Team GB, with the equestrian eventing team and mountain biker Tom Pidcock defending their Olympic titles. Versatile rider Pidcock has added world and European mountain bike titles since becoming Olympic champion in superb style in Tokyo, while Britain's eventers are also seeking individual glory, as well as successive team gold medals, for the first time since 1972.
