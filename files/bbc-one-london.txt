BBC One London
Today, June 23
Clare Balding presents live coverage of the final from the prestigious Queen's Club tournament in west London. With Wimbledon just a week away, this is the perfect grass court warm-up, and its roll call of winners is littered with some of the greatest players in history. Carlos Alcaraz claimed his first grass court title here in 2023, and the Spanish sensation built on that success by claiming the Wimbledon singles title just a few weeks later.
