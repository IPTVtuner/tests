Live action from Huddersfield as England take on Tonga in the second Test of the first international series between the nations. After the opening game at St Helens, all eyes will be on the John Smith's Stadium as Shaun Wane's side tussle again with a powerful Tongan side packed with NRL players as well as Super League stars such as St Helens' Konrad Hurrell and the Giants' Tuimoala Lolohea, who could feature on his home pitch.
