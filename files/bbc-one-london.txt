BBC One London
Today, August 04
Isa Guha and Mark Chapman recap all the sporting drama of day nine in Paris, with the men's 100m final taking centre stage on a busy day of Olympic competition. The star attraction on the track was American Noah Lyles, while British record holder Zharnel Hughes was hoping to add an Olympic medal to his World Championship bronze. Team GB athletes were also competing in the swimming this evening, with the men vying to improve on their Tokyo silver in the 4x100m medley relay.
