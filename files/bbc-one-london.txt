JJ Chalmers and Jeanette Kwakye are across all the morning's action in the French capital, with Team GB seeking another equestrian crown at the magnificent Palace of Versailles. Great Britain has a formidable stable of riders and horses in Paris, and the dressage is no exception. Carl Hester is appearing at his seventh games, and Lottie Fry is the reigning individual freestyle and individual special world champion.
