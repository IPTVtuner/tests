Today, August 04
A pulsating evening is guaranteed in Paris on day nine as the world's fastest athletes line up for one of the most anticipated and greatest events in sport, the men's 100m final. America's global superstar Noah Lyles is the reigning world champion and the man to beat. British hopeful Zharnel Hughes will seek to exorcise the memory of his disqualification at Tokyo 2020, while Botswana's world silver medallist Letsile Tebogo is another strong contender.
