Today, June 06
Newly-appointed Minister for Social Affairs, Hugh Abbot, is on the way to deliver a press launch for his new policy, the `Snooper Force', when it is quashed by Malcolm Tucker, Number 10's Chief Political Advisor. Hugh and his advisors must then scramble to brainstorm a new cost-free policy that they have to announce to the nation's press in 40 minutes.
