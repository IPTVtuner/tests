The story of one of Britain's best-loved comedy actors, who found fame in the 1970s in `Rising Damp' and `The Fall and Rise of Reginald Perrin'. Although best known for comedy roles, his career began in the theatre and he appeared in many dramas on TV and film. Friends and family talk about his life and career, including Joan Collins, Don Warrington, Sue Nicholls, John Barron and Eric Chappell.
