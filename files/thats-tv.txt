Another of the world's elite fighting forces in World War II is our subject: the Commandos, created by Churchill in the summer of 1940 as a means of striking at the coasts of Nazi-occupied Europe. Starting out as a mere raiding force, they became the spearhead of the Allied amphibious landings in North Africa, Sicily, Italy and Normandy. Without them, our modern society would be a different one indeed.
