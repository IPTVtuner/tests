Today, March 07
Joanna Lumley reaches Khartoum, the capital of Sudan and the confluence of the Blue Nile and the White Nile. She observes the city's famous whirling dervishes, and enjoys the `dukhan' - a traditional beauty treatment for women. Joanna also takes a detour high into Ethiopia's Simien mountains where she encounters an unusual group of Olympic hopefuls.
