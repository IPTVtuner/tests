That's TV
Today, November 18
Second of a two-part programme in which Trevor McDonald takes viewers on a journey into the machinations of the American mafia. Trevor travels to Philadelphia, where he explores one of the bloodiest periods in the long history of the mob, as he sits down with Ralph Natale, the first ever mafia godfather to speak on television. In an extraordinary interview, Natale spells out just how ruthless a boss must be to control the men under him.
