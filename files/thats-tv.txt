Today, May 10
U2, who, with more than 170 million record sales worldwide, are one of the best-selling music groups of all time, but who are also well-known for their philanthropy and charity work. Over twenty years, vocalist Bono has made a name for himself as a worker for social and economic change in poorer countries. This programme tells the story of the band's energy, drive and commitment to social causes and their efforts to use their influence to bring change to people who need it the most.
