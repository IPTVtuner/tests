Brazos Kane is a gunslinger who renounces his violent ways and goes to work for land baron who has two daughters. One, the virtuous Jane, falls for Kane; the other, bad-girl Bess, falls for a hired gun Bard Mackey. As a vicious range war breaks out, Kane finds himself on one side with Mackey on the other and has to decide if he'll break his vow never to pick up a gun again.
