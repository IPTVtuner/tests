A brooding psychological drama where the female of the species is deadlier than the male. Vienna is a saloon owner whose plans to build a railway station anger the locals. For protection, she calls on her ex-lover Johnny Guitar, once a psychotic gunslinger, now trying to lead a new life as a saloon singer. They face Emma Small, who leads the town gang with her lover and gunslinger, the Dancin' Kid.
