The multi award winning period drama Downton Abbey returns on TV3 for a fifth, promising even more twists and turns, high-stakes drama, laugh-out-comedy and romance, played out by some of the most iconic characters on television. Set in 1924, the series will continue some of the storylines established in series four, including Mary Crawley's relationship with Anthony Foyle and Charles Blake, Tom Branson's flirtation with Sarah Bunting, and Edith Crawley's pregnancy.
