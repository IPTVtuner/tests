Drama series looking at life in a fashionable London household at the turn of the 20th century. It's summer 1919, James and Georgina are the only remaining house members at Eaton Place, while Hudson and Mrs Bridges are considering retirement. When Richard and Virginia return from their honeymoon and James asks them to move back in, they initially decline - until Virginia's children spend an afternoon at the house.
