The second round of the 2024 MotoGP World Championship comes from Portimao with the Portugal GP Sprint - in a prelude to the Portugal GP Race itself, riders will compete over 12 laps of the Autodromo Internacional do Algarve. Jorge Martin won the opening Sprint race of the season in Qatar and the Spaniard will be hoping to add another to his unrivalled ten Sprint victories.
