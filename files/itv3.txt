Jill Douglas presents coverage of the 2024 British Open from the Centaur in Cheltenham, the only tournament on the World Snooker Tour to feature an open draw - this session features the conclusion of the final played over the best of 19 frames. Mark Williams won this tournament for the second time in three years in 2023 and will be one of the favourites in a field that features some of the biggest names in the sport each vying for the first prize of 100,000 pounds.
