The `Inspector Morse' prequel `Endeavour' won such big ratings when it aired on ITV in 2012, the network is giving fans more in the form of six film-length episodes. The series follows the relationship - both working and personal - between young DC Endeavour Morse and his senior partner, DI Fred Thursday. Returning actors include Shaun Evans as Endeavour and Roger Allam as Thursday, while Sean Rigby takes on the role of a young PC Strange - the character who appeared in the original `Morse' series, played by James Grout - and Abigail Thaw, the daughter of the first Inspector Morse, John Thaw, make further guest appearances as Dorothea Frazil.
