Jill Douglas presents coverage of the 2024 British Open from the Centaur in Cheltenham, the only tournament on the World Snooker Tour to feature an open draw - this session features action from the quarter-finals played over the best of nine frames. Mark Williams won this tournament for the second time in three years in 2023 and will be one of the favourites in a field that features some of the biggest names in the sport each vying for the first prize of 100,000 pounds.
