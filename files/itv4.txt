Dean Macey and Adam Rooney go on a road trip across England trying their luck on a diverse trio of venues. Challenge one gets underway on the River Trent, where they target barbel alongside guest England international angler Matt Godfrey, before switching their attentions to carp on the Grand Union Canal. For their final challenge, Dean and Adam go head to head, teaming up with monster carper Tom Dove and England International angler Steve Ringer, at Earlswood Lake, in an all out match.
