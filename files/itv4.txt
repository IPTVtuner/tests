Judy Murray speaks to Hayley Turner, the most successful woman in British horse riding history, about her life and career. From career threatening injuries to battling prejudice towards female riders, Hayley tells of the driving forces that motivate her on a daily basis and how she has been a trailblazer for levelling the field in her sport. Featuring contributions from Frankie Dettori, Sir AP McCoy, Ed Chamberlin, Hollie Doyle and Rachael Blackmore.
