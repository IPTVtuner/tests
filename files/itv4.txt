Action from the 14th round of the season as riders look to secure vital championship points at the Motul Grand Prix of Japan, which is taking place at the Mobility Resort Motegi. Located amongst the vast natural beauty of the northern Kanto district, the twin ring circuit at Motegi consists of a 1.5-mile oval and a 2.9-mile road course constructed to international standards - built by Honda as the ultimate test facility in August 1997, the road circuit first became home to MotoGP in 1999.
