Round Two of the 2025 British Rally Championship is the Carlisle Stages Rally - this rally has been added to the 2025 calendar and replaces the cancelled Severn Valley Stages. The rally offers 50 miles of competitive action in well-known stages such as Kershope and Ash Park and also incorporates scenic finish celebrations in front of the picturesque Kielder Castle.
