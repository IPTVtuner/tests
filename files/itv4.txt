The World Series of Darts heads to Hamilton in New Zealand - Rob Cross was crowned champion in 2023 when he beat Nathan Aspinall, he is competing once again alongside some of the best players in the game including world champion Luke Humphries and teenage sensation and Premier League champion Luke Littler. The first day sees eight local players take on eight of the world's best for a place in the quarter-finals - commentary comes from Stuart Pyke, Dan Dawson, and Mark Webster.
