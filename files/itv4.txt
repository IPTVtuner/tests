Matt Barbet presents coverage from the second stage of the 2024 Lloyds Bank Tour of Britain Men with commentary from Ned Boulting and David Millar - this stage sees the Tour head to Darlington for the first time since 2009 for a 152-kilometre (94-mile) race through the Tees Valley and North Yorkshire to Redcar. The stage crosses the Cleveland Hills and heads through the North York Moors National Park to the coast at Robin Hood's Bay via a series of short, sharp climbs - turning north via Whitby.
