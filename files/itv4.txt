Round Five of the 2025 World Rally Championship is the WRC Vodafone Rally de Portugal - a founding round of the WRC back in 1973, the rally is ranked as one of its classic events with challenging gravel stages to the east and south of Porto. Varying road surfaces and changeable weather add to the task facing crews, but it's also extremely popular among fans with enthusiastic spectators following the action in their tens of thousands.
