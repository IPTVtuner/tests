Jill Douglas presents coverage of the 2023 Champion of Champions tournament from Bolton - one of snooker's most prestigious events, it features 16 champions from the past 12 months all vying to be crowned as Champion of Champions. This session features action from the conclusion of the final that is due to be played over the best of 19 frames - studio analysis comes from Stephen Hendry, Alan McManus, Ken Doherty and Neal Foulds, with commentary from David Hendon and Phil Yates.
