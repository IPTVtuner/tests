After crossing the globe with events in Europe, New York, Bahrain, Australia and New Zealand, the 2024 World Series of Darts concludes with the Finals in Amsterdam. The field has been increased to 32 players this year and this first session will see eight first-round matches played out - Jacqui Oatley is joined by Chris Mason, Alan Warriner-Little and Mark Webster for coverage with commentary from John Rawling, Stuart Pyke and Ned Boulting.
