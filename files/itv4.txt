Brian Moore and Jim Rosenthal present football highlights from April 1981. As the season drew to a close, Ipswich Town were looking to maintain their title challenge as they entertained Manchester City at Portman Road. Elsewhere, Tottenham took on Liverpool at White Hart Lane and, in a pivotal game at the bottom of the first division, Sunderland faced Brighton and Hove Albion at Roker Park.
