Vicki Butler-Henderson and Alex Riley are tasked with championing the best car to mobilise the world after World War 2 to be released in 1948. Vicki gets to grips with the iconic Citroen 2CV, while Alex makes a case for the British workhouse the Series 1 Land Rover. Making the all important decision are former Top Gear Stig and Hollywood stunt driver Ben Collins, journalist and presenter Erin Baker and classic car specialist and auctioneer Nick Wells.
