Hugh Woozencroft presents coverage of New Zealand's final pool game at the 2023 Rugby World Cup in France as the three-time champions take on Uruguay at the OL Stadium in Lyon - Uruguay are playing in their fifth Rugby World Cup, but this is the first time that they have been drawn in the same pool as the All Blacks. Studio analysis comes from Sean Fitzpatrick, Lawrence Dallaglio and Jim Hamilton, and commentary is provided by Johnnie Hammond, Nolli Waterman, and Ugo Monye.
