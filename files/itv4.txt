Extended highlights of the 1988-89 Division One title decider between Liverpool and Arsenal. George Graham's North London side travelled to Anfield on the final day of the season needing to win by two goals to lift the trophy, producing one of the most dramatic and memorable endings to a season ever. Elton Welsby was joined in the studio by the England manager Bobby Robson, while Brian Moore and David Pleat were in the commentary box, with Jim Rosenthal reporting.
