Jill Douglas presents coverage of the 2024 Champion of Champions event from Bolton - one of snooker's most prestigious tournaments, it features 16 champions from the past 12 months all vying to be crowned Champion of Champions. A host of top names are all set to compete including world champion Kyren Wilson, Ronnie O'Sullivan, Judd Trump, Mark Allen and 2024 British Open winner Mark Selby.
