Jill Douglas presents coverage of the 2023 Champion of Champions tournament from Bolton - one of snooker's most prestigious events, it features 16 champions from the past 12 months all vying to be crowned as Champion of Champions. A host of top names are all set to compete - including world champion Luca Brecel, Barry Hawkins, Mark Williams, and Jimmy White - with Ronnie O'Sullivan looking to defend his title and player of the season Judd Trump hoping for revenge after his final defeat in 2022.
