Vicki Butler-Henderson and Alex Riley each make a case for a supercar released in 1975 that would go on to become an 80s icon. Vicki drives and champions the Ferrari 308 made famous by the Magnum PI TV series, while Alex gets behind the wheel of one of his childhood heroes, James Bond car the Lotus Esprit. Opinion and the selection of a winning car comes from a panel of former Top Gear Stig Ben Collins, broadcaster and journalist Jason Barlow and author Richard Porter.
