Paul is in the northernmost spur of Skye, the beautiful Trotternish Peninsula. The Isle of Skye is an intoxicating mix of all that is great about Scotland's islands, and Paul begins his adventure at one of the star attractions of this part of the island, The Old Man of Storr. His follows in the footsteps of the dinosaurs who once walked this land, through the enchanted fairy glen and out to the seldom-visited Fladda Chuain islands.
