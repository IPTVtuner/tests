In June 2016, 20-year-old Brit Michael Sandford was arrested at a Donald Trump rally, after trying to take a police officer's gun in a bid to shoot the then republican presidential nominee. We follow Michael's family as they travel to the US for his sentencing, unsure of when they might see him again. We explore Michael's complex past while using exclusive eyewitness interviews and never seen before archive to piece together the elaborate assassination plot.
