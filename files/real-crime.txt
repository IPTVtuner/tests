Today, June 13
It's July 14, 1997, and Gianni Versace, probably the most famous fashion designer in the world, is kicking back at his Florida mansion, but in just 24 hours he will be dead. With exclusive interviews with his intimates, combined with archive footage and dramatic reconstruction, `Final 24' maps out Versace's last day alive and the dramatic events that led to his brutal murder at the hands of a serial killer.
