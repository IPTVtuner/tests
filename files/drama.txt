Today, November 06
As Edinburgh prepares to play host to the World Trade Summit, an unidentified body is found in the grounds of the hotel housing the conference. Soon after, development minister Ben Webster is found dead beneath the ramparts of Edinburgh Castle - did he jump, or was he pushed? As Rebus begins preliminary investigations, he is warned off the case by the S012 commander. Forging ahead anyway, he is intent on discovering the truth.
