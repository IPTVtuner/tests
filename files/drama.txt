When local businessman Daniel Boden is found stabbed to death at the wheel of his car, DC Dangerous Davies discovers he was caught up in a lucrative insurance scam set up by a secret gentleman's club. The only clue the team find is a record of a phone call the victim made shortly before he died to a man called John Mosshead. As DC Davies tries to discover the man's identity, he discovers that he is being followed.
