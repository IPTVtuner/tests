The brutal murder of 16-year-old Brianna Ghey in a Warrington park shocked the nation - in this powerful documentary, her mother Esther talks openly about her beautiful and fearless daughter and how the tragedy has driven her fight for change. With exclusive police access, the film details the 28-hour search that led to the arrest of her 15-year-old killers - Scarlett Jenkinson and Eddie Ratcliffe - as never-before-seen custody interviews show how they tried to cover their tracks.
