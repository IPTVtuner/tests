Alan Titchmarsh and his team head to Derbyshire to build an exciting garden for sprightly D-Day Veteran Jack Parrott and his wife of 68 years Margaret. With advice on patio-size greenhouses and clever ways to create raised beds - the team come up with a daring design and create a new accessible garden that allows the soon-to-be-100 war hero - to once again indulge in his life-long love of gardening.
