The tenth Rugby World Cup gets under way in Paris as the hosts France take on three-time winners New Zealand in a mouth-watering opening game - the French have pulled off some memorable World Cup wins against the formidable All Blacks in 1999 and 2007, and coach Fabien Galthie has a wealth of talent at his disposal this time around, not least former World Player of the Year Antoine Dupont and the dynamic Gregory Alldritt in the pack.
