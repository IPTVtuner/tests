In 1964, Granada Television brought together a group of seven-year-olds for a groundbreaking TV programme. For over half a century we've watched their lives unfold. To celebrate the arrival of the latest instalment in the longest-running documentary series in TV history, 63 Up, famous fans of the show explain why it means so much to them. They also give a personal insight into how the programme has reflected the major milestones in their lives.
