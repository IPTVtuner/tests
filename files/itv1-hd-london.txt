In the spring of 2023, the Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam opened its doors to the largest Vermeer exhibition in history - the show sold out within days of going on sale. This documentary invites us to a privileged view of the exhibition of the century accompanied by the director of the Rijksmuseum and the curators of the show - experience the genius of Vermeer and explore his fascinating and mysterious life with leading world experts.
