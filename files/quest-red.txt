In 2009, York chef Claudia Lawrence mysteriously disappeared, leading to one of the most high-profile missing person cases the United Kingdom has ever seen. Following the anniversary of her disappearance, Claudia's parents, alongside her friend Jen and local journalist Mike Laycock, relive the past decade and talk about the police investigation and review, which led to nine arrests and interviews under caution but no convictions.
