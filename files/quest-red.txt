Davina McCall and Nicky Campbell with more remarkable and moving stories of people anxious to find missing family members. At 39, Peter Gunn has struggled to come to terms with being given up for adoption by his birth mother when he was a baby. Although he was brought up by a loving family, a sense of abandonment contributed to his going down the path of addiction. Having put his life back on track, he still wants to hear why his birth mother acted as she did.
