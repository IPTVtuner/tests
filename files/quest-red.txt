Quest Red
Today, October 07
Davina McCall and Nicky Campbell return with the series that aims to reunite family members after years of separation and offer a lifeline for people who are desperate to find long-lost relatives. Former rugby professional Mark Appleson, 44, hopes to uncover the truth about his past and find his birth mother. The show also follows Stella Stanley, who was persuaded to give up her baby daughter for adoption in the 1960s.
