In Somerset, upcycling expert Sarah Moore explores Keynsham Recycling Centre hoping to discover unwanted items that can be revamped or revitalised and sold on for profit. Sarah enlists the help of screen print designer Daniel Heath and clock restorer Paul Parry to rejuvenate an old writing bureau and a 1940s mantle clock, while Sarah tries to cultivate something sellable from fragrant bags of garden waste.
