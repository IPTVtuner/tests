Gordon visits Bazzini restaurant in Ridgewood, New Jersey. Owner Paul Bazzini was an award-winning chef in New York who decided to run his own restaurant but he never realised how difficult the change would be. Now, he finds everyday tasks overwhelming and his passion for food is in danger of being extinguished by the daily grind. However, passion isn't the only problem in this failing restaurant.
