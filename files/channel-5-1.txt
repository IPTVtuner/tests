Perennial family favourite about Dorothy, a young girl from the American Midwest, who is swept by a cyclone to a magical kingdom over the rainbow. There she meets a scarecrow, a tin man and a cowardly lion, who join her on a quest to find the all-powerful Wizard of Oz, the only man who can help her get back to to Kansas. However, the Wicked Witch of the West has other plans for Dorothy and her new friends.
