Up to a million gladiators are thought to have died in arenas across the Roman Empire. Apart from ancient Italy itself, Roman Britain had the highest density of purpose-built gladiatorial arenas in Europe. Interest in gladiators has been at an all-time high since Russell Crowe's sword and sandals epic. This programme recreates the world of the Roman arena and how six gladiators lived, fought and died.
