The case of Charlie Graves is a race against the clock. Having recently passed away, his close friends got in touch with the Heir Hunters in the hope they could track down family members in time to attend his funeral. In delving into his family tree Michael Buerk and the Heir Hunters discover Charlie worked in the printing presses and was involved in one of the most protracted and bitter industrial disputes in British history.
