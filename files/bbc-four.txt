In 2002, a daring raid by outlawed spiritual group Falun Gong hacks into state TV stations in China in an attempt to counter the government narrative about their practice. Celebrated comic book artist Daxiong revisits the audacious plot he took part in. In the aftermath of the hack, police raids sweep Changchun City, and Daxiong is forced to flee. He arrives in North America, blaming the hijacking for worsening a violent repression.
