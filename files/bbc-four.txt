Justina, an indigenous woman, has been rewarded by her former employer for her lifelong work as a maid with an enormous, crumbling mansion deep in the Argentine Pampas. The only condition is that she can never sell it. Justina, aged 60, and her lesbian daughter Alexia, aged 20, move from the city to this desolate paradise and eke out a living.
