Heir to the throne, Prince Hal, defies his father, King Henry, by spending his time at Mistress Quickly's tavern in the company of the dissolute Falstaff and his companions. The King is threatened by a rebellion led by Hal's rival, Hotspur, his father Northumberland and his uncle Worcester. Considering danger to the state, Hal joins his father to defeat the rebels at the Battle of Shrewsbury and to kill Hotspur.
