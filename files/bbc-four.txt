Live coverage of the Pet Shop Boys headline set at `BBC Radio 2 Live in Hyde Park'. Neil Tennant and Chris Lowe are the most successful duo in UK music history, having achieved 42 top 30 singles in the UK since 1985, so there's no shortage of hits for them to choose from as they play their only UK gig of the year, in front of more than 40,000 festival-goers.
