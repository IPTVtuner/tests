Hot off the heels of her Olivier Award win, actress Romola Garai shares her experiences of filming the much-loved 2009 series Emma. The four-part Austen adaptation was a stunning take on a much-loved classic, and Romola looks back on her role as the titular character. Keen to make Emma more relatable and loveable, Romola remembers how she drew on the text to bring Austen's complicated heroine to life.
