Friends, family and co-stars share their memories of Elizabeth Taylor, the Hollywood icon who died in March 2011. Famous for her many husbands, love of diamonds and her AIDS fundraising, the actress who won two film Oscars and was awarded an honorary Oscar for her humanitarian work, is celebrated by Liza Minnelli, Joan Collins, Pierce Brosnan, Angela Lansbury, Sir David Frost, Larry King and many others.
