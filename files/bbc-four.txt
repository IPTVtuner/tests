Clive Myrie presents a celebration of one of the most iconic voices of the 20th century, Sarah Vaughan. Featuring some of the songs that Vaughan brought to fame, including Misty, If You Could See Me Now and Body and Soul. Sarah, or 'Sassy' as she was known, would have celebrated her 100th birthday in 2024. Her spellbinding voice defied the boundaries of a single genre, working alongside the likes of Dizzy Gillespie, Count Basie, and Michael Tilson Thomas.
