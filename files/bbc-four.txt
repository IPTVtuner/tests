The final stage of the journey is through Arctic Europe. He travels to a remote Russian village, deep within the vast boreal forest that stretches for thousands of miles across the top of the world. Bruce then travels into Norway where he lives with the Saami, reindeer herders who use snow machines, boats and even helicopters to move their animals. The last stop is a remote community in the Svalbard archipelago.
