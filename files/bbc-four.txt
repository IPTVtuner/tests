Documentary filmmaker Norma Percy looks back on her acclaimed 1994 series 'Watergate', reflecting on her approach and the challenges she faced when piecing together in forensic detail the tale of the botched break-in that sent shock waves through America and the world. Norma explains how she gained exclusive access to the figures who went from obscurity to notoriety due to the Watergate scandal, and she shares her feelings on why the series may still resonate with viewers.
