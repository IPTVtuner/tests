For nearly 20 years, folk group Calan have been thrilling audiences all over the world with their unique take on Welsh traditional music. From Malaysia to the USA, the band have amassed a loyal following of fans with their modern versioning of traditional folk songs, combining foot-tapping rhythms with catchy melodies and some electrifying playing. In this special concert for St David's Day, the band are joined by the BBC National Orchestra of Wales under the baton of Grant Llewelyn.
