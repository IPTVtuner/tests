Always one of the most exciting Proms of the summer, the brightest young stars in classical music take over the stage of the Royal Albert Hall as the National Youth Orchestra of Great Britain make their annual trip to the Proms. Expect a night of high drama and passionate emotion as these exciting young musicians explore the uniting power of music and love, with music from Mahler, Wagner, Missy Mazzioli and a new commission from Dani Howard. Linton Stephens presents joined by a special guest.
